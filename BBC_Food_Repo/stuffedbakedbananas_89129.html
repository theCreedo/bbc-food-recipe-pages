Stuffed baked bananas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Levi Roots
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas , in their skin
  • 85g/3oz dark chocolate , broken into pieces
  • 3 tbsp dark rum
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Make two long incisions though the skin along each length of the banana and open up slightly. Push the chocolate pieces inside. Place the bananas in an ovenproof frying pan, pour over the rum and transfer to the oven to bake for 5-7 minutes, until golden.

  3. Serve the bananas with a scoop of ice cream each on top.
