Pithivier with leek and cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 55g/2oz ready-rolled puff pastry , cut into two small disks
  • 20g/¾oz blue cheese
  • 15g/½oz leek , chopped
  • 1 free range egg , beaten
  • fresh parsley leaves, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Place one pastry disk on a baking sheet and top with the leeks and blue cheese, leaving a 1cm/½in border all around.

  3. Brush the clean pastry border with the beaten egg and top with the second pastry disk. Press down the edge to seal the pastry disks, and brush all over the top with the remaining egg.

  4. Place into the oven to bake fo r8-10 minutes, or until golden and risen.

  5. To serve, place onto a warmed plate and garnish with the parsley.
