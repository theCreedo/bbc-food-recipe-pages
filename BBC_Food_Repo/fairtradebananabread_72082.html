Fairtrade banana bread

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

1 to 2 hours

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

Vegetarian

Whip up this tasty banana bread in four easy steps for a healthy tea-time treat.

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz self-raising flour
  • 100g/4oz butter
  • 150g/5oz caster sugar
  • 450g/1lb Fairtrade bananas (the softer the better), peeled and mashed
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 175g/6oz mixed dried fruit

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Mix all the of the ingredients except the dried fruit together. You can do this in a food processor or by hand in a mixing bowl.

  3. When the ingredients are all thoroughly combined, fold in the dried fruit.

  4. Spoon the batter mixture into a 1kg/2lb non-stick loaf tin, spread it out evenly and bake it for one and a half hours.

  5. When the banana bread has risen, golden-brown on top and cooked through, remove it from the oven and set aside to cool on a wire rack. Slice before serving.
