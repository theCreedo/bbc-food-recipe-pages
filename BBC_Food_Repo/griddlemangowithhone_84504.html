Griddled mango with honey and chilli

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ mango , stone removed
  • 20ml/1½ tbsp clear honey
  • ¼ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 15g/¼oz butter

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to its highest setting.

  2. Cut into the mango flesh in a criss-cross fashion with a sharp knife, then push the skin inside out, to expose the maximum mango flesh.

  3. Drizzle with the honey and sprinkle over the chilli flakes.

  4. Melt the butter in an ovenproof frying pan and add the mango to the pan, flesh side down. Cook for two minutes, then turn over. Transfer to the grill and cook for a further 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown.
