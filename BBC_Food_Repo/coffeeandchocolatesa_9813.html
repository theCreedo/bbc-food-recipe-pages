Coffee and chocolate sauce

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Heston Blumenthal
From Veg Talk

Ingredients

  • 340g/12oz unrefined sugar
  • 570ml/1 pint water
  • 40 coffee beans
  • 180g/6oz cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 60g/2oz of the best quality bitter chocolate you can find

Method

  1. Place the sugar in a pan over a medium heat. Brush the sides of the pan with a pastry brush dipped in a mug of cold water. Continue this process until the sugar caramelises. (You will probably end up using a third of the water).

  2. Add the rest of the water, coffee beans, cocoa powder, salt and chocolate.

  3. Bring to the boil.

  4. Pass through a very fine sieve and allow to cool.

  5. Serve cold.
