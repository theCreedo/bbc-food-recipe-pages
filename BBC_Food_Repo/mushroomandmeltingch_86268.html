Mushroom and melting cheese tart

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pastry

  • 170g/6oz plain flour
  • 100g/3½oz butter
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 2 tbsp ice cold water
  • pinch salt

For the filling

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 85g/3oz mixed mushrooms , sliced
  • 100g/3½oz Camembert , chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 3 tbsp double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the pastry, place the flour and butter into a food processor and pulse until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.

  3. Crack in the egg and add the water and salt and blend again until the mixture comes together as a dough.

  4. Turn out onto a floured surface and roll out to the thickness of a pound coin. Line a small tart tin with the pastry and trim away the excess. Refrigerate for five minutes.

  5. Line the tart with baking parchment and fill with baking beans. Transfer to the oven and bake for ten minutes. Remove the baking beans and parchment.

  6. For the filling, heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and gently fry for 3-4 minutes.

  7. Add the garlic and mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes.

  8. Spoon the mushroom mixture into the pastry case. Top with the Camembert, thyme and cream.

  9. Transfer to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the pastry is golden-brown and the cheese is beginning to melt.

  10. To serve, remove from the tart tin and place onto a serving plate.
