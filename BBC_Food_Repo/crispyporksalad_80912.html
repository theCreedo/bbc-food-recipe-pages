Crispy pork salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pork

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 70g/3oz pork mince
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 limes , juice only
  • pinch sea salt
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander

For the salad

  • handful each of basil, coriander , parsley and dill
  • oil , to drizzle

Method

  1. For the pork, heat the oil in a pan until very hot and add the pork with the spices and lime juice and fry for 8 minutes until golden and crispy.

  2. Stir through the coriander and take off the heat.

  3. For the salad, mix together the herbs and drizzle with oil.

  4. To serve, place the salad on a plate and sprinkle over the pork.
