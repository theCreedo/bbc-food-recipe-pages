Steamed salmon with stir fried green beans and fragrant herb rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the steamed salmon

  • 150g/5oz salmon fillet
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the stir-fried green beans

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ onion , finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic , chopped
  • 85g/3oz green beans , cooked
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • squeeze lemon juice , to taste

For the fragrant herb rice

  • 200g/7oz basmati rice
  • 1 large handful fresh coriander , chopped
  • 1 large handful fresh chives , chopped
  • 1 large handful fresh dill , chopped
  • 1 small handful fresh watercress , chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the steamed salmon, place the salmon into a bamboo steamer set over a wok half-filled with boiling water. Squeeze over the lemon, add the white wine and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Cover tightly with a lid and steam for 6-7 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.

  2. For the stir-fried green beans, heat the olive oil in a wok, add the onion and fry for 3-4 minutes, or until softened but not coloured. Add the garlic, green beans and soy sauce and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, or until the green beans are cooked with still a bit of crunch, then squeeze over the lemon juice, to taste.

  3. For the fragrant rice, place the rice into a pan of boiling salted water and cook for 8-10 minutes, or until the rice is tender. Drain well and then stir in the chopped herbs and watercress and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. To serve, pile the rice on to a serving plate, spoon over the stir-fried green beans and top with the steamed salmon.
