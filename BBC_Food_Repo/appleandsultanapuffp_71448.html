Apple and sultana puff pastry diamond with clotted cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the compote

  • 3 Bramley apples - peeled, cored and diced
  • 1 vanilla pod - split lengthways and seeds scraped out
  • 50g/1¾oz butter
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • pinch of sugar
  • 100g/3½oz sultanas
  • splash of water

For the diamond

  • 200g/7¼oz puff pastry
  • egg wash
  • pinch of sugar
  • clotted cream , to serve

Method

  1. For the compote, put the apples, vanilla, butter, cinnamon, sugar and water in pan and cook, stirring often, until ingredients turn into a purée.

  2. Then mix in the sultanas and set aside.

  3. For the diamond, roll out the puff pastry and cut two 10cm/4in wide diamonds.

  4. Cut around the edges of the diamonds and fold the corners back on each other.

  5. Brush with egg wash, sprinkle with sugar and bake at 180C/350F/Gas 4 until golden brown. Leave to cool.

  6. Slice the diamonds in half, spoon the purée over the bottom, add the pastry lids and serve with clotted cream.
