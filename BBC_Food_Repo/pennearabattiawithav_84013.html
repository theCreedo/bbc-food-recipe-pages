Penne arrabiata with avocado and rocket salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pasta

  • 125g/4½oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • ¼ red onion , finely chopped
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée

For the salad

  • ½ avocado , stone removed, sliced
  • small handful rocket leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tbsp balsamic vinegar

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients for the pasta into a serving bowl, mixing well to combine the ingredients.

  2. For the salad, place all the ingredients on a separate bowl, mixing together well. Serve alongside the bowl of pasta.
