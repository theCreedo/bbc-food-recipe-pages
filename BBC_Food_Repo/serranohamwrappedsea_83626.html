Serrano ham-wrapped sea bass with sautéed potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sautéed potato

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz new potato , par-boiled for five minutes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh chives

For the sea bass

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz sea bass fillet
  • 2 slices Serrano ham
  • ½ lemon , juice only

Method

  1. For the sautéed potato, heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat.

  2. Cut the potatoes in half and add to the pan. Sauté for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until crispy golden.

  3. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and sprinkle over the chives.

  4. For the sea bass, heat the olive oil in a clean frying pan.

  5. Season the sea bass on both sides with salt and freshly ground black pepper, then wrap the fillet in the Serrano ham.

  6. Add to the pan fry for three minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

  7. Squeeze over the lemon juice and remove from the heat.

  8. To serve, place the sautéed potatoes in the middle of a warm plate and place the sea bass on top.
