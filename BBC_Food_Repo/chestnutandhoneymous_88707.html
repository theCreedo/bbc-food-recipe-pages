Chestnut and honey mousse, dusted with cinnamon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 300ml/10fl oz double cream
  • 100g/3½oz chestnuts , out of their shells
  • 200ml/7fl oz milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • pinch ground cinnamon
  • 2 fresh mint sprigs

Method

  1. In a bowl, whip the cream until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed.

  2. Place the chestnuts and milk into a food processor and blend until smooth. Fold this mixture into the cream, along with half of the honey.

  3. To serve, spoon the mixture into two serving glasses. Drizzle with the remaining honey, dust with cinnamon and garnish with a sprig of mint.
