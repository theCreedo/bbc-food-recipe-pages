Beef with fritto misto and chilli sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Thin strips of steak in a spicy dressing with delicious deep-fried vegetables.

By Theo Randall
From 2007-16

Ingredients

For the beef and chilli sauce

  • 2x 175g/6oz sirloin steaks
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red chilli , finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh flatleaf parsley
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed with a little sea salt
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar

For the fritto misto

  • vegetable oil , for frying
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 300g/11oz plain flour
  • 1 litre/1¾ pints warm water
  • 1 free-range egg white
  • 2 violet artichokes , tough outer leaves and choke removed
  • 2 Jerusalem artichokes , peeled, sliced
  • 1 small head radicchio , outer leaves removed, quartered
  • 4 fresh sage leaves
  • salt

Method

  1. For the beef and chilli sauce, heat a griddle pan until very hot.

  2. Season the steaks with salt, freshly ground black pepper and the chopped rosemary.

  3. Brush with two tablespoons of the olive oil and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side (for medium), then remove from the pan and set aside to rest for 10 minutes.

  4. Mix the red chilli, parsley, garlic, vinegar and remaining tablespoon of olive oil together in a bowl.

  5. Slice the steaks and spoon over the chilli sauce.

  6. For the fritto misto, heat the oil in a deep-fat fryer to 180C/350F. Do not leave the deep-fat fryer unattended.

  7. Place the flour into a large bowl, make a well in the centre and pour in the olive oil. Slowly whisk in the warm water until the batter is the consistency of runny honey. (You may not need all the water.)

  8. Whisk the egg white in a separate bowl until soft peaks form when the whisk is removed. Fold the egg white into the batter.

  9. Add the prepared vegetables to a bowl along with a ladleful of batter and mix until well combined.

  10. Cook the vegetables in the deep-fat fryer for 2-3 minutes, or until crisp. Remove from the fryer with a slotted spoon, set aside to drain on kitchen paper and sprinkle with salt. (You may need to cook the vegetables in batches.)

  11. To serve, arrange the beef on serving plates and place the fritto misto alongside.
