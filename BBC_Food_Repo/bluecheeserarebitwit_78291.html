Blue cheese rarebit with pickled cabbage

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 2

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread , toasted
  • 1 tomato , sliced
  • 50g/1¾oz Dolcelatte cheese
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

For the pickled cabbage

  • ¾ red cabbage , shredded
  • 55ml/2fl oz white wine vinegar
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar

Method

  1. Pre-heat the grill to high.

  2. Place the cabbage in a pan with the sugar and white wine vinegar. Bring to the boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 12 minutes.

  3. Place the toast on a baking sheet. Place the sliced tomatoes on top, sprinkle with cheese and drizzle with the Worcestershire sauce.

  4. Place under the preheated grill for four minutes, or until the cheese has melted.

  5. Serve the rarebit on a plate with some of the pickled cabbage to the side.
