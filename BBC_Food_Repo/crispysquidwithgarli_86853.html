Crispy squid with garlic mayonnaise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2-4

An easy starter that everyone will love. Feel free to add other seasoning into your coating or mayonnaise.

Ingredients

For the crispy squid

  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp semolina or polenta
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 200g/7oz squid (tubes and tentacles) when defrosted, cut into rings
  • vegetable oil , for deep-frying

For the garlic mayonnaise

  • ½ garlic clove, crushed with salt, to taste
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise

Method

  1. For the crispy squid, mix the cornflour, semolina or polenta and paprika in a bowl.

  2. Dip the squid rings and tentacles into the mixture, a few at a time, to coat completely.

  3. Heat the oil into a deep, heavy-based saucepan until a small cube of bread sizzles and turns golden when dropped into it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended.) When the oil is hot enough, fry the squid in batches for 1-2 minutes, flipping them once, until golden-brown and crisp. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen paper.

  4. Meanwhile, for the garlic mayonnaise, stir the crushed garlic into the mayonnaise.

  5. To serve, place the crispy squid onto a serving plate with the mayonnaise in a small bowl alongside.
