Olly’s sham-pagne

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 2

Dietary

Vegetarian

Cutting back on calories? You don't have to give up the bubbly at a social occasion with this elderflower mocktail.

By Olly Smith
From Good Food Friday with Michel Roux Jr and Olly Smith

Ingredients

  • 60ml lime cordial
  • 60ml elderflower cordial
  • 3 drops orange blossom water
  • 250ml ice cold soda water

Method

  1. Pour all the ingredients into a jug and give it a stir. Serve in champagne flutes to blend in at a party.
