Roasted spiced pineapple

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • ½ lemon , juice
  • ½ medium pineapple , peeled, cored and quartered
  • pinch of cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp powdered ginger
  • whipped cream , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Melt the butter, sugar and lemon juice together in an ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat. Add the pineapple chunks, stirring well to coat.

  3. Add the cinnamon and ginger, and place into the oven for 5-10 minutes until the pineapple has softened and browned.

  4. To serve, place the pineapple in a bowl and add a dollop of cream.
