Cheesy 'risotto'

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ red onion , peeled and chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled and chopped
  • 140g/5oz long-grain rice , cooked according to instructions on packet
  • handful spinach leaves
  • 110g/4oz Stinking Bishop cheese
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • olive oil for drizzling

Method

  1. Heat the oil in large non-stick frying pan, add the onion and garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes, to soften.

  2. Add the cooked rice and stir in well to coat in the oil.

  3. Add the spinach leaves, stir into the rice mixture and allow to wilt.

  4. Add the cheese and salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste. Heat through for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. To serve, transfer the risotto to a warm dish and drizzle with olive oil.
