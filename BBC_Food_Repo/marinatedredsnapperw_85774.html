Marinated red snapper with turmeric and coconut rice salad and citrus relish

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the marinated red snapper

  • 1 fillet red snapper , skinned and sliced into very thin strips
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lime , juice only
  • 1 lemon , juice only

For the turmeric and coconut rice salad

  • 100g/3½oz rice , cooked according to packet instructions and cooled
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tbsp coconut milk
  • small handful fresh basil , chopped
  • small handful fresh mint , chopped
  • ¼ onion , finely chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the citrus relish

  • 1 lemon , juice only
  • 1 lime, zest only
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • small piece fresh coconut , chopped flesh only
  • 1 tomato , seeds removed and sliced
  • 1 orange , peeled and chopped
  • 1 tbsp coconut milk
  • small handful fresh flatleaf parsley , chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tomato , sliced, to serve

Method

  1. For the marinated red snapper, place the raw snapper pieces into a bowl and sprinkle with salt. Add the lime and lemon juice and stir. Set aside to marinate for a few minutes.

  2. For the rice salad, place all the rice salad ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  3. For the citrus relish, place all the citrus relish ingredients into another bowl and mix well.

  4. To serve, place the tomato slices onto a serving plate. Top with the rice salad, citrus relish and marinated snapper and drizzle over the marinade.
