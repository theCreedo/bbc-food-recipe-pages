Spiced white chocolate and hazlenut samosas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 12

These filo parcels are gently spiced with cardamom, cloves and cinnamon.

By Rav
From The Great British Bake Off

Ingredients

For the pastry

  • 270g packet ready-made filo pastry (6 sheets)
  • large knob of unsalted butter , melted
  • icing sugar , for dusting

For the filling

  • 120g/4¼oz white chocolate , broken into pieces
  • 50g/1¾oz blanched hazelnuts
  • ½ tsp green cardamom seeds, ground
  • ¼ tsp ground cloves
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp chilli powder

Method

  1. For the filling, pulse the white chocolate and hazelnuts in a food processor until finely chopped. Tip into a bowl and add the cardamom, cloves, cinnamon and chilli powder. Mix well and set aside.

  2. Preheat the oven to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4 and line two baking trays with greaseproof paper.

  3. For the pastry, cut the filo into strips about 20x5cm/8x2in. Brush one side of the strips lightly with melted butter.

  4. Place a teaspoon of the chocolate filling at one end of a pastry strip. Take the corner and fold diagonally, enclosing the filling and forming a triangle. Fold again along the upper crease of the triangle. Keep folding in this way until you reach the end of the strip. Brush the outer surface with more butter. Place on a baking tray and cover with a tea towel while you make the rest of the samosas.

  5. Bake the samosas for 6 minutes, then turn them over and bake for another 4 minutes, or until golden-brown. Dust with icing sugar before serving.
