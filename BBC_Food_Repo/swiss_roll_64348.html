Swiss roll

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes 5 Swiss rolls

Dietary

Vegetarian

A teatime favourite to feed 40 people. You could add whipped cream to the filling to make them extra special.

By The Hairy Bikers
From Hairy Bikers' Meals on Wheels

Ingredients

  • 20 free-range eggs
  • 500g/1lb 2oz caster sugar , plus extra to dust
  • 500g/1lb 2oz self-raising flour
  • 750g/1lb 10oz strawberry jam
  • icing sugar , to dust

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5. Grease and line five Swiss roll tins.

  2. Beat the eggs and sugar in a large bowl until pale and tripled in volume.

  3. Sieve the flour into the mixture and stir very gently until just combined.

  4. Pour the mixture into the lined tins and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes or until golden-brown all over.

  5. Turn the sponges out and allow to cool a little on a wire rack. Peel off the greaseproof paper.

  6. Spread the top of the sponge with jam then carefully roll up the sponge, starting with the short side, until you have a log shape.

  7. Sit the log so that the seam is on the underside and dust with icing sugar.
