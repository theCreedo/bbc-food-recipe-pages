Pea-breaded hake with sun-dried tomato salsa

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sun-dried tomato salsa

  • 25ml/1oz sun-dried tomato oil (the oil from a jar of sun-dried tomatoes )
  • 1 clove garlic , finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped red onion
  • pinch dried chilli flakes
  • 50g/2oz sun-dried tomatoes

For the hake

  • 50g/2oz fresh peas
  • 50g/2oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • 100g/3½oz hake
  • 50g/2oz plain flour
  • 1 free-range egg , beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. To make the tomato salsa, heat the oil in a frying pan and cook the garlic, onion and chilli until soft.

  3. Add the tomatoes and cook for five minutes.

  4. To make the hake, place the peas and breadcrumbs in a food processor and blend together.

  5. Toss the hake in the flour, dip in the egg then roll in the pea crust until coated all over.

  6. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and fry the hake for one minute on each side until coloured.

  7. Place in the oven and roast for five minutes or until cooked through. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  8. Serve the hake in the middle of a warm plate and spoon the salsa around.
