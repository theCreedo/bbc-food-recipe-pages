Swiss muesli

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

Vegetarian

This is a great healthy breakfast that you can make in large quantities to last the week.

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Food and Drink

Ingredients

  • 170g/6oz rolled oats
  • 170g/6oz raisins or sultanas
  • 110g/4oz dried apricots , roughly chopped
  • 110g /4oz hazelnuts , split in half and roasted
  • 350ml/12fl oz fresh apple juice
  • 2 apples (coxes or granny smiths) peeled and coarsely grated
  • 1 small tub Greek or natural yoghurt
  • 4 ripe apricots , roughly chopped
  • clear honey to serve

Method

  1. In a large bowl mix together the oats, raisins, apricots and hazelnuts. Pour over the apple juice and leave for 10-15 minutes to allow the oats and dried fruit to soak up the juice.

  2. Divide the muesli between 6 glass bowls and top with the grated apple. Spoon on a dollop of yoghurt and drizzle over some honey. Serve immediately.
