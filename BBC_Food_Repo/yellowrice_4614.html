Yellow rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Lee Cox

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 400g/14oz basmati rice
  • 750m/1¼ pint boiling water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • pinch of saffron soaked in a little warm water
  • 30g/1¼oz soaked raisins (optional)

Method

  1. Heat the butter and oil in a deep saucepan until they start to foam. Add the rice and stir until all the grains are coated. Pour in the boiling water and stir. Add the salt and the saffron. Bring the water back to the boil. Add the raisins.

  2. Reduce the heat to a very gentle simmer and cover the pan with a tight fitting lid. Cook until all the water has been absorbed (about 20 minutes). Turn off the heat and leave the rice to stand for 5 minutes before serving.
