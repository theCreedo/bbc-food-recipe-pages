Veggie-lovers bagel

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian

Avocado gives this sandwich its creamy richness, and the healthy fats will help to keep you feeling full. Pack as many raw veggies as you can on top. Best made fresh.

By Emily Angle

Ingredients

  • 1 wholemeal or 50/50 thin bagel , sliced in half
  • ½ small avocado (about 45g/ /1½oz), sliced
  • 4 radish es, thinly sliced
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 30g/1oz roasted red pepper from a jar (not in oil), drained, sliced
  • small handful rocket
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp chilli sauce

Method

  1. Pile all the veggies onto the bagel, season with salt and pepper and dot over the chilli sauce.
