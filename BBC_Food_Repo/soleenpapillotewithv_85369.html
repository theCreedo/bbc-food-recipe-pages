Sole en papillote with vegetables

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the sole en papillote

  • 2 fillets sole , skinned
  • 2 baby leeks , very finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • 30g/1oz butter
  • 1 lemon , sliced
  • small handful fresh chives , roughly chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the vegetables

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 110g/4oz Chantenay carrots (or other small carrots ), tops trimmed, halved lengthways
  • 4 baby leeks , trimmed
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • 1-2 tbsp water
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the sole en papillote, place each sole fillet onto a large sheet of greaseproof paper. Sprinkle over the chopped leeks, drizzle with white wine and dot with butter. Place the lemon slices onto the fish, sprinkle over the chives and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Bring the greaseproof paper up around the fish and seal the edges to make two parcels. Place onto a baking sheet and transfer to the oven to bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.

  4. For the vegetables, heat the oil and butter in a pan over a medium heat and fry the carrots and leeks for 2-3 minutes.

  5. Add the coriander seeds and white wine and simmer until reduced slightly.

  6. Add 1-2 tablespoons of water and simmer for another 2-3 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper, add the herbs and stir to combine.

  7. To serve, place the sole parcels onto plates with the vegetables.
