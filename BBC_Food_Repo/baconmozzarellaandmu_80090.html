Bacon, mozzarella, and mushroom pizza

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz plain flour
  • 50ml/2fl oz water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • pinch salt

For the tomato sauce

  • 1 tin plum tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 25g/1oz parsley chopped
  • 25g/1oz basil chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the topping

  • 50g/1¾oz crispy fried bacon lardons
  • 50g/1¾oz smoked mozzarella ripped
  • 50g/1¾oz marinated mushrooms

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. To make the base, in a large bowl mix the flour, water, oil and salt to form a dough.

  3. Dust your hands with flour and shape the dough into a ball. Roll out on a floured surface into an 20cm/8in round.

  4. Heat a small frying pan and cook for two minutes on each side.

  5. To make the tomato sauce, place the tomatoes, olive oil and garlic in a saucepan.

  6. Simmer until thick, then stir in the herbs. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  7. Spread some of the tomato sauce on the pizza. Sprinkle over the bacon, mushrooms and cheese, and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  8. Bake in the oven for five minutes until the cheese has melted.
