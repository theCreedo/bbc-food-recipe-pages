Pork chop with sweet and sour tomatoes, mustard sauce and savoury rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pork chop

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pork chop
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 150ml/5½fl oz double cream
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • splash white wine
  • small handful fresh sage , chopped

For the sweet and sour tomatoes

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tomato , chopped
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp tomato ketchup
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the savoury rice

  • 200g/7oz basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil , plus extra for greasing
  • 1 fig , chopped
  • ½ tomato , chopped
  • small handful fresh basil , chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the pork, heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add the pork, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry until golden-brown on both sides.

  2. Add the cream, mustard and white wine and simmer gently for five minutes, or until the sauce has thickened slightly. Remove from the heat and keep warm.

  3. For the tomatoes, heat the olive oil in a pan, add the onion and fry for 3-4 minutes over a gentle heat, until softened but not coloured. Add the garlic and fry for one minute.

  4. Add the chopped tomato, red wine vinegar and ketchup and simmer for five minutes. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. For the rice, cook the rice according to the packet instructions and then drain.

  6. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the cooked rice, chopped fig, tomato, basil, salt and freshly ground black pepper and stir well over a low heat until warmed through.

  7. To serve, pack the rice into a lightly greased ramekin, then turn out onto a serving plate. Place the pork chop next to the rice and pour over the mustard sauce from the pan. Spoon the sweet and sour tomatoes on the side and serve.
