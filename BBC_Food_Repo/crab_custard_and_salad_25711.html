Baked crab custard pots

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Savoury crab custard pots topped with fresh, white crab salad. A lovely light starter for supper with friends.

By Michel Roux Jr.
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz brown crab meat
  • 4 free-range egg yolks
  • 1 tsp freshly ground nutmeg
  • 200ml/7fl oz milk
  • 60ml/4 tbsp double cream
  • 125g/4½oz white crab meat
  • 1 spring onion , thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon , juice only
  • 400g/14oz small tomatoes ,quartered
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped micro basil , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C/140 Fan/Gas 3.

  2. In a bowl, beat together the brown crab meat, egg yolks, nutmeg, milk and cream. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Pass the mixture through a sieve into a clean, large bowl, pressing all of the liquid out of the crab meat.

  4. Pour the mixture into four ramekin dishes. Bake for 20 minutes, or until set. It will depend on the size of the ramekin. Leave to cool.

  5. In a clean bowl, toss the white crab meat with the spring onion and olive oil.

  6. Place the tomatoes around the outside of the custard pots, spoon over the white crab meat mixture, squeeze over the lemon, sprinkle with the micro basil and serve.
