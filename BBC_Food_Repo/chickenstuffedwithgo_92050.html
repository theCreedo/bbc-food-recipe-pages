Chicken breasts stuffed with goats' cheese wrapped in Parma ham

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Gordon Jones
From Virgin Cooks

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts , preferably free-range, skin on
  • 250g/8¾oz goats' cheese
  • bunch fresh mixed herbs (such as parsley, tarragon and dill), chopped
  • 8 slices Parma ham
  • freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 500g/1lb 2oz carrots , steamed until tender
  • 2 bundles English asparagus , steamed until tender

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Using a sharp knife, make a deep incision down one side of each chicken breast to form a pocket in each.

  3. In a bowl, mix together the goats' cheese and mixed herbs, then stuff the chicken breasts with the mixture.

  4. Wrap two slices of Parma ham around each stuffed chicken breast and season, to taste, with freshly ground black pepper.

  5. Place the stuffed, wrapped chicken breasts onto a baking tray and transfer to the oven. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. (NB: The chicken is cooked through when the juices run clear when the chicken is pierced in the thickest part with a skewer.)

  6. To serve, place one stuffed chicken breast into the centre of each plate. Serve with steamed carrots and asparagus.
