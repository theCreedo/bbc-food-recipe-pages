Prunes with orange and vanilla yoghurt

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5¼oz Greek yoghurt
  • 1 vanilla pod , seeds scraped out
  • ½ can of tinned prunes with juice
  • ½ orange , juice only
  • sprig fresh mint , to garnish

Method

  1. Place the yoghurt with the vanilla seeds into a bowl and mix together well.

  2. Place the prunes and juice into a dessert glass.

  3. Add the orange juice and spoon over the vanilla yoghurt.

  4. Garnish with the sprig of fresh mint and serve.
