Knickerbocker glory

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Makes 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 digestive biscuits
  • 55g/2oz walnuts
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 orange , peeled and sliced
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone cheese
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream , whipped
  • 110g/3½oz tinned cherries, drained, syrup reserved

Method

  1. Blend the biscuits, walnuts, and honey together in a small food processor.

  2. Mix the mascarpone and double cream together.

  3. Spoon half of the biscuit mixture into the base of a wine glass. Place half of the orange slices on top, then half of the cherries and half of the cream mixture. Repeat this with the remaining ingredients and finish with cream on the top.

  4. Reduce six tablespoons of the cherry syrup by half in a pan over a high heat. Leave to cool then drizzle over the top of the cream and serve immediately.
