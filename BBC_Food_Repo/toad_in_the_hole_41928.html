Toad in the hole for a crowd

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 40

This feeds a lot of people. If you are using a domestic oven you will have to use four roasting tins and cook in two batches.

Ingredients

  • 20 free-range eggs
  • 1.25kg/2lb 12oz plain flour
  • 500ml/18fl oz whole milk
  • 1.5 litres/2 pints 14fl oz cold water
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 300g/10½oz lard
  • 40 pork sausages

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Place the eggs, flour, milk, water and seasoning in a large mixer and whisk until you have a smooth batter. Place in the fridge to rest for 30 minutes.

  3. Put the lard in two large roasting tins and place in the oven for 5-8 minutes or until it is smoking.

  4. Carefully pour in the batter - it will sizzle softly in the hot fat - then arrange the sausages in the batter.

  5. Transfer the tins back to the oven and bake for 25-30 minutes until puffed and golden-brown.
