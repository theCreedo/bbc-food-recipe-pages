Linguine with spicy tomatoes and broccoli

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Garrey Dawson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic , chopped
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 6 cherry tomatoes , halved
  • ½ head broccoli , cut into florets
  • splash white wine
  • splash boiling water
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 100g/3½oz linguine

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the onion and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until softened but not coloured. Add the garlic and curry powder and fry for one minute, then stir in the cherry tomatoes and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until softened.

  2. Add the broccoli florets, a splash of white wine and a splash of boiling water and simmer for 3-4 minutes, or until the broccoli is just tender. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Cook the linguine, according to packet instructions, then drain. Add the cooked linguine to the sauce and stir well to coat.

  4. Pile the linguine into a bowl and serve.
