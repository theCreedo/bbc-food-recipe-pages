Pan-fried frittata with spaghetti and salmon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz spaghetti , cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 55g/2oz skinless salmon fillet, chopped
  • small bunch fresh parsley , chopped
  • 3 free-range eggs , beaten
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • fresh chives , chopped, to serve

Method

  1. Place the spaghetti, parmesan, salmon and parsley into a bowl and add the eggs. Mix well to coat and season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Heat the oil in a 15cm/6in non-stick frying pan and add the egg mixture. Cook for 3-4 minutes, until the underside is golden brown.

  3. Turn the frittata over by placing a large plate over the pan to cover it, then carefully turn over the pan to catch the frittata. Carefully return the frittata to the pan uncooked-side down and continue to cook for a further 2-3 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through.

  4. Serve onto a clean plate, garnished with chives.
