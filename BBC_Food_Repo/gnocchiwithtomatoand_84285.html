Gnocchi with tomato and basil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , chopped
  • 1 garlic , clove
  • ½ tsp red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp caster sugar
  • 150ml/4½fl oz passata
  • small bunch basil , chopped
  • 125g/4oz ready-made gnocchi , cooked according to packet instructions
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and fry until softened.

  2. Add the vinegar and sugar and stir until the sugar has dissolved.

  3. Add the passata, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat to simmer for 5-6 minutes.

  4. Add the basil and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. Add the gnocchi and stir well to coat.

  6. To serve, place the gnocchi and sauce into a bowl and serve immediately.
