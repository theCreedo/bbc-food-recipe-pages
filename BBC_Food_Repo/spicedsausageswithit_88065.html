Spiced sausages with Italian-style roast potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the Italian-style roast potatoes

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 baking potato , peeled and chopped into chunks
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary , chopped roughly
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed roughly with the flat side of a large knife
  • 1 tsp sea salt

For the spiced sausages

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 pork sausages
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes

To serve

  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 small sprig fresh rosemary

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. For the potatoes, heat the olive oil in an ovenproof frying pan and fry the potatoes for 8-10 minutes, or until they start to colour. Add the rosemary, garlic cloves and salt. Place the pan in the oven and roast until the potatoes are cooked through, about 8-10 minutes.

  3. For the sausages, heat the olive oil in a frying pan and add the sausages. Add the chilli flakes and cook over a medium heat, turning often, for about 10-15 minutes, or until completely cooked through.

  4. To serve, place the sausages on one side of a serving plate and the rosemary potatoes on the other. Sprinkle with cayenne pepper and garnish with a sprig of fresh rosemary.
