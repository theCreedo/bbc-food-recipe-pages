Totally tropical Eton mess

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian

The classic summer dessert gets a tropical makeover with mango and passion fruit. Using ready-made meringues means this sweet and zingy version is ready in minutes.

By Georgina Mackenzie

Ingredients

  • 300ml/10fl oz double cream
  • 200ml/7fl oz Greek-style yoghurt
  • 4 shop-bought meringue nests
  • 1 ripe mango , skin and stone removed, flesh cut into 1cm/½in pieces
  • 3 passion fruits , halved
  • 4 fresh mint sprigs, to decorate (optional)

Method

  1. Gently whip the cream in a large bowl until soft peaks form.

  2. Fold the yoghurt through the cream, being careful not to over-mix.

  3. Break the meringues into the bowl and gently stir to combine. It should be quite a stiff mixture.

  4. Spoon a layer of the meringue mixture into the base of four serving glasses. Sprinkle over a handful of mango pieces and drizzle with passion fruit seeds. Repeat until all the meringue mixture and fruit is used up.

  5. Decorate with mint sprigs, if using) and eat immediately.
