Crumble-topped tarts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian

Little lemon tarts are great to make with kids and take no time at all. You can use jam instead of lemon curd for a change.

Ingredients

  • 125g/4½oz shortcrust pastry
  • 4 rounded tsp lemon curd
  • 30g/1oz butter
  • 2 tbsp rolled oats
  • 2 dessertspoons caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp self-raising flour , plus extra for dusting

Method

  1. Wash your hands and put on an apron. Weigh and measure all the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 5.

  2. Roll out the pastry onto a lightly floured surface and cut out four circles using a 8cm/3¼ in cutter. Line four holes in a muffin tin with the pastry circles.

  3. Spoon the lemon curd into the pastry cases.

  4. Melt the butter in a small saucepan and leave it to cool a little. Next add the oats, sugar and flour to the butter and mix with a fork, breaking it up into crumbs.

  5. Sprinkle the crumbs onto the top of the tarts. Place the muffin tin onto a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes. Serve with fresh raspberries or strawberries.
