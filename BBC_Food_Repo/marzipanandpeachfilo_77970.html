Marzipan and peach filo crown

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 x 100g/3½oz/12-inch squares of ready-made filo pastry
  • 2 x 100g/3½oz/12-inch square slices marzipan
  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter , melted
  • 2 peach halves
  • icing sugar for dusting

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas6

  2. Roll the marzipan squares to 1cm/½in thickness.

  3. Place the two 12-inch squares of layered filo pastry onto a floured surface. Brush each pastry square with melted butter.

  4. Place one marzipan square in the centre of each pastry square.

  5. Place a peach half flat-side down on top of each marzipan square.

  6. Fold in the corners, making sure each peach in the centre is visible.

  7. Brush with the melted butter and transfer to a baking tray. Bake for six minutes.

  8. To serve, place on a plate with a dusting of icing sugar.
