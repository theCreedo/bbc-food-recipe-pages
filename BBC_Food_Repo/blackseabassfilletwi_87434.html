Black sea bass fillet with spicy broccoli rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the spicy broccoli rice

  • 150g/5½oz basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 fresh red chilli , finely chopped
  • 100g/3½oz broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

For the sea bass

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 sea bass fillet
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives , to serve

Method

  1. For the spicy broccoli rice, cook the rice in boiling salted water, according to packet instructions. Drain.

  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan. Add the chilli and broccoli and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes. Add the cooked rice, parsley and soy sauce and stir together. Spoon the rice onto a serving plate and keep warm.

  3. For the sea bass, heat the oil in another frying pan. Place the sea bass fillet into the pan, skin-side down, and fry for three minutes. Add the balsamic vinegar, sesame seeds and honey. Turn the fish over and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the fish is completely cooked through.

  4. To serve, place the sea bass on the plate next to the rice and sprinkle over the chopped fresh chives.
