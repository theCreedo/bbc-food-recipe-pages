Prawns and purple sprouting broccoli with chilli and lemon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 16 king prawns , peeled
  • 300g/10½oz purple sprouting broccoli , blanched and refreshed in cold water
  • 2 canned anchovies, drained and chopped
  • 1½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 garlic clove, sliced
  • 1 lemon , juice only
  • 1 head pak choi , finely sliced
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • ½ tsp freshly grated nutmeg

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a wok until very hot. Add the prawns and stir-fry for three minutes.

  2. Add the purple sprouting broccoli, anchovies, chilli flakes and garlic and stir-fry for a further 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the lemon juice, pak choi and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Stir-fry for 30 seconds then transfer to four serving bowls.

  4. Sprinkle each dish with sesame seeds and a little freshly grated nutmeg and serve.
