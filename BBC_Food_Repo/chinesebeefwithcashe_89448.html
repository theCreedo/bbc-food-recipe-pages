Chinese beef with cashews and soy chilli dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz sirloin steak
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 red chilli , finely sliced
  • ½ lime , juice only

To serve

  • 100g/3½oz rice noodles , cooked according to packet instructions

Method

  1. Rub the olive oil into the steak and season with the freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Heat a frying pan over a high heat. Add the steak and fry for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking. Remove and set aside in a warm place to rest.

  3. Add the soy sauce, honey, chilli and lime to the pan the steak was cooked in and mix thoroughly. Cook over a medium heat for 2-3 minutes until the sauce has reduced to a sticky glaze.

  4. To serve, cut the steak in half. Place the cooked rice noodles onto a serving plate, arrange the slices of steak on top, and pour the juices from the pan all over.
