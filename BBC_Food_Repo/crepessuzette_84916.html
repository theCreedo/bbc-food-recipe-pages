Crêpes suzette

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the crêpes

  • 150g/7oz plain flour
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 75ml/2½fl oz whole milk
  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • ½ clementine , juice only
  • 25g/1oz butter

To serve

  • ½ clementine , chopped
  • 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt

Method

  1. For the crêpes, place the flour, eggs, milk, double cream and clementine juice into a bowl and mix to make a smooth batter.

  2. Heat half of the butter in a non-stick frying pan, place a ladleful of batter into the pan, swirl around to coat the base of the pan and cook for one minute on each side, or until golden-brown. Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining mixture to make a second pancake.

  3. To serve, place the pancakes onto a plate with the chopped clementine and a dollop of Greek yoghurt.
