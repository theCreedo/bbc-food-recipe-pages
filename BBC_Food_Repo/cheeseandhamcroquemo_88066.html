Cheese and ham croque monsieur

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white crusty bread
  • 100g/3½oz Brie , sliced
  • 2 slices ham
  • 2 free-range eggs , beaten
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 15g/½oz butter

To serve

  • oakleaf and lamb's lettuce leaves
  • drizzle olive oil

Method

  1. Take one slice of bread, lay the slices of Brie on top and then cover with the ham. Place the second slice of bread on top, then dip the sandwich into the beaten egg, coating well.

  2. Heat the oil and butter in a frying pan and fry the sandwich until golden-brown on both sides.

  3. To serve, cut in half and arrange on a serving plate. Garnish with lettuce leaves and drizzle with olive oil.
