Sausage palmiers

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2-4

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet ready-rolled puff pastry
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten with a pinch of salt
  • 115g/4oz chipolatas
  • 25g/1oz ready-cooked stuffing
  • 6 tbsp ready-made cranberry sauce

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/450F/Gas 7 and preheat a non-stick baking sheet.

  2. Brush the sheet of puff pastry with the beaten egg.

  3. Remove the sausages from their skins and spread the sausagemeat thinly over the pastry sheet.

  4. Cut the stuffing into thin slices and scatter on top.

  5. Brush the edges of the pastry with egg, then roll up tightly. Trim off the ends and then cut into thin slices, pressing down to flatten them slightly.

  6. Place the palmiers on the preheated baking sheet and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the filling is cooked through and the pastry is lightly golden.

  7. Place the cranberry sauce into a small pan and heat gently.

  8. To serve, arrange the sausage palmiers on a plate and top with dollops of the hot cranberry sauce.
