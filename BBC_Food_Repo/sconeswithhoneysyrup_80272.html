Scones with honey syrup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the scones

  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter , cut into cubes
  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 50g/1¾oz quark (or similar fresh soft cheese )
  • 25ml/1oz ginger syrup, from a jar of stem ginger

For the honey syrup

  • 50ml/2fl oz runny honey
  • 1 vanilla pod , seeds scraped out
  • ½ orange , juice only

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Place a baking sheet in the oven to preheat.

  2. To make the scones, place the flour, butter, sugar, quark and ginger syrup into a food processor and blend until the mixture comes together as a dough.

  3. Using floured hands, knead the dough lightly on a floured surface and pat out to about 2cm/¾in thick.

  4. Using a 5cm/2in pastry cutter, cut out four scone rounds.

  5. Place the scone rounds onto the preheated baking sheet and place into the oven to bake for eight minutes, or until risen and golden.

  6. To make the honey syrup, place a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the honey, vanilla pod and seeds and orange juice. Stir to mix together and heat to simmer for 2-3 minutes, until it reaches a thick, syrupy consistency.

  7. To serve, place the scones onto a plate with the syrup drizzled over.
