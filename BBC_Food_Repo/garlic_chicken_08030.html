Garlic chicken

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 1-2

This delicious casserole uses whole garlic cloves to add an extra kick of flavour - the result is surprisingly subtle.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • ¼ orange pepper , seeds removed
  • 1 cooked chicken breast , skin removed, sliced
  • 4 new potatoes
  • 2 heaped tbsp canned red kidney beans , rinsed and drained
  • 3 whole garlic cloves, skins left on
  • 1 tsp plain flour
  • pinch paprika
  • pinch vegetable bouillon powder
  • 125ml/4½fl oz boiling water
  • ½ tsp yeast extract, such as Marmite or Bovril
  • steamed green beans , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/450F/Gas 7.

  2. Place the pepper, chicken, potatoes and kidney beans into a lidded casserole dish. Add the garlic cloves, with their skins still on. Sprinkle over the flour and paprika.

  3. Add the bouillon powder to the water, then mix in the yeast extract until dissolved. Pour this stock mixture into the casserole dish to just cover its contents. Cover the dish with the lid.

  4. Cook the garlic chicken in the oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender. Serve with steamed green beans.
