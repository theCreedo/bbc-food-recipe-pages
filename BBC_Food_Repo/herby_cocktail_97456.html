Herby cocktail

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 8

Dietary

Vegetarian

Fragrant lemon verbena and perfumed orange blossom combine for a sublimely refreshing cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 200g/7oz caster sugar
  • 1 lemon , zest and juice
  • 6 lemon verbena leaves, lightly bruised, plus extra for serving
  • 1 tsp orange blossom water
  • 400ml/14fl oz soda water
  • 100ml/3½fl oz vodka
  • ice

Method

  1. To make a sugar syrup, put the sugar, lemon zest and juice, and 200ml/7fl oz water in a large saucepan placed on a medium-low heat. Warm until the sugar has dissolved.

  2. Mix together the sugar syrup, bruised verbena leaves, orange blossom water, soda water and vodka in a jug.

  3. Pour into glasses filled with ice and finish with lemon verbena leaves.
