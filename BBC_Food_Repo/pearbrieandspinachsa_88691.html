Pear, Brie and spinach salad with croûtons

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the dressing

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 55g/2oz Brie , chopped
  • ½ pear , chopped

For the salad

  • 85g/3oz spinach
  • ¼ red onion , sliced thinly
  • ½ pear , cut into chunks
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 thick slice bread , crusts cut off, cut into chunks
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp roughly chopped fresh rosemary , to serve

Method

  1. For the dressing, place all the ingredients in the bowl of a small food processor and process until smooth.

  2. For the salad, place the spinach, red onion and pear into a bowl. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix gently to combine.

  3. Drizzle the bread chunks with the olive oil and heat a frying pan until hot. Fry the bread until golden-brown on all sides.

  4. To serve, pile the salad onto a plate and scatter the croutons on top. Drizzle over the Brie and pear dressing and garnish with chopped fresh rosemary.
