Cherry cheesecake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made macaroon biscuit
  • 125g/4oz quark cheese (or other soft cheese )
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream , whipped
  • 100g/3½oz tinned cherries
  • 1 sprig fresh mint
  • icing sugar , for dusting

Method

  1. Place the macaroon biscuit at the base of a 7cm/3in chefs' ring.

  2. Place the cream and the quark into a bowl and mix together well.

  3. Place the cream and cheese mixture onto the top of the biscuit in the chefs' ring and place into the fridge for ten minutes to set.

  4. Remove from the fridge and place onto a serving plate and release the cheesecake from the chefs' ring by warming the sides of the ring with a mini-blowtorch.

  5. To serve, top the cheesecake with cherries. Garnish with a mint sprig and a dusting of icing sugar.
