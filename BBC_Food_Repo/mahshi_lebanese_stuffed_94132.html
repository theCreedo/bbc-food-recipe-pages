Mahshi (Lebanese stuffed vegetables)

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6–8

Make vegetables taste even more amazing with a spiced lamb and rice stuffing.

By The Ayoubi Family
From The Big Family Cooking Showdown

Ingredients

For the stuffing

  • 200g/7oz uncooked pudding rice
  • 250g/9oz lamb mince
  • good pinch saffron
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp ghee or vegetable oil

For the vegetables

  • 8 small courgettes , cored
  • 8 baby aubergines , cored
  • 6 mini peppers , cored
  • 1 bulb garlic , cloves separated
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 2 fresh mint sprigs, leaves picked
  • 1 tbsp ghee or vegetable oil

To serve

  • 2 spring onions , finely chopped
  • 1 small bunch fresh coriander , finely chopped
  • 1 small bunch fresh parsley , finely chopped
  • 1–2 fresh mint sprigs, leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 120g/4½oz rocket
  • 8 pitta breads , toasted

Method

  1. To make the stuffing, mix the ingredients together in a large bowl.

  2. To make the vegetables, stuff the cored courgettes, aubergines and peppers with the stuffing.

  3. Put the ghee into a large lidded saucepan and lay the stuffed vegetables in a single layer if you can. Tuck the garlic cloves in and around.

  4. Mix the tomato purée with 800ml/1pint 8fl oz warm water and the mint, then pour into the pan. Cover with a lid, bring to the boil then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 40 minutes.

  5. Serve the stuffed vegetables and the cooking juices with the spring onions, herbs and rocket scattered over the top and the pitta breads on the side to soak up the juices.
