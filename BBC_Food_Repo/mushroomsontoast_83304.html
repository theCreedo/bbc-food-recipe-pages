Mushrooms on toast

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 1 clove garlic , finely chopped
  • ¼ red onion , finely sliced
  • 100g/3½oz mushrooms , chopped in half
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp chopped flatleaf parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 thick slices toast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • pinch paprika , to garnish

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a large frying pan, then fry the garlic and onion until soft.

  2. Add the mushrooms and cook until soft.

  3. Add the double cream and parsley then season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and reduce until thick.

  4. To serve, drizzle the toast with the olive oil and spoon over the mushroom mixture. Garnish with a pinch of paprika.
