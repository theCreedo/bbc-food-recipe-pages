Seared tuna with radish salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

This Asian-style tuna salad would make an impressive starter for a dinner party.

By James Martin
From 2007-16

Ingredients

For the tuna

  • 4 x 100g/3½oz fresh tuna steaks
  • 1 tbsp yuzu juice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

For the salad

  • 16 radish es, very finely sliced
  • ¼ mooli, very finely sliced with a vegetable peeler
  • ½ cucumber , cut into small balls
  • 4 tbsp fresh mint leaves
  • 4 tbsp fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 lime , juice only
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds , toasted

Method

  1. For the tuna, heat a griddle pan until very hot.

  2. Sear the tuna for 30 seconds on each side, then remove from the pan.

  3. Cut the tuna into 5mm/¼in thick slices and arrange on a large serving plate.

  4. Mix the yuzu juice, soy sauce and olive oil together in a bowl and pour it over the tuna. Set aside to marinate for a few minutes.

  5. For the salad, mix the radishes, mooli, cucumber, mint and coriander together in a bowl.

  6. Whisk the lime juice, soy sauce and olive oil together then drizzle it over the salad and mix until well combined.

  7. Sprinkle over the sesame seeds and mix together.

  8. To serve, spoon the salad alongside the sliced tuna and serve immediately.
