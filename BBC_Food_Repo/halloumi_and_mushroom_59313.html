Halloumi and mushroom kebabs with salsa verde and couscous

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian

Cook this quick and colourful vegetarian meal from The Hairy Bikers; it would taste great on the barbecue too.

By The Hairy Bikers
From The Hairy Bikers' Cook Off

Ingredients

For the couscous

  • 125g/4½oz couscous
  • 125ml/4½fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon , juice only
  • 1 ready-roasted red pepper , shredded
  • 50g/2oz toasted flaked almonds
  • 50g/2oz dried apricots , chopped

For the skewers

  • 200g/7oz halloumi cheese, cut into 2.5cm/1 inch cubes
  • 8 cherry tomatoes
  • 8 button mushrooms
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

For the salsa verde

  • bunch parsley , chopped leaves only
  • bunch basil chopped, chopped leaves only
  • bunch mint chopped, chopped leaves only
  • 2 tbsp drained capers
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • 100ml/3½fl oz olive oil
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to season

Method

  1. For the couscous, place the couscous into a bowl and cover with the hot stock. Cover the bowl with a plate or cling film and leave for four minutes.

  2. Uncover the bowl and fluff up the couscous with a fork. Stir in the remaining ingredients, season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. For the kebabs, preheat a griddle pan over a medium-high heat. Toss all of the kebab ingredients together and season to taste with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper. Thread the ingredients onto metal kebab skewers and cook on the hot griddle for 5-6 minutes, turning frequently.

  4. For the salsa verde, place all of the salsa verde ingredients, apart from the olive oil, into a food processor and blend, gradually adding the oil until you have a loose paste.

  5. To serve, spoon the couscous onto plates, top with the kebabs and drizzle with the salsa verde.
