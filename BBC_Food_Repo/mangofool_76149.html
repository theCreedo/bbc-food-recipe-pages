Mango fool

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • ½ mango , cut up
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 lime, zest and juice only, (keep separate)
  • 300ml/10½fl oz double cream
  • fresh mint , to garnish

Method

  1. Purée the mango in a blender with the caster sugar and lime juice.

  2. Whip the double cream with the lime zest.

  3. Take a tall glass and start by layering with the mango purée and lime cream. Repeat this process and finish with the mango purée on top.

  4. Garnish with the mint to serve.
