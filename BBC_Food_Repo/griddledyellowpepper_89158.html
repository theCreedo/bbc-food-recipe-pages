Griddled yellow pepper with rocket and sesame salad and skordalia

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Maria Elia
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the griddled pepper

  • 1 yellow pepper , de-seeded, cut into quarters
  • olive oil , for drizzling
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the rocket and sesame salad

  • 1 large handful rocket leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds , toasted
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the skordalia

  • 2 slices white bread , crusts removed
  • 3 tbsp cold water
  • 1 clove garlic , chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ lemon , juice only
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the griddled pepper, drizzle the pepper quarters with olive oil and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Heat a griddle pan until smoking, add the peppers and griddle for 3-4 minutes, or until golden-brown and slightly charred.

  2. For the rocket salad, mix all of the salad ingredients together in a large bowl.

  3. For the skordalia, place the bread in a bowl with the water and leave to soak for a few minutes. Squeeze out the excess water, then place the softened bread into a food processor with the rest of the skordalia ingredients and blend to a smooth paste.

  4. To serve, spoon the skordalia onto two serving plates, top with the griddled pepper and serve the rocket salad on the sides.
