Beef stir-fry

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the beef stir-fry

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ½ red onion
  • 1 tomato , cut into wedges
  • ½ broccoli stem, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp fresh ginger , grated
  • 150g/5½ oz beef , sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Method

  1. To make the stir-fry, heat the vegetable oil in a hot wok. Add the red onion and cook for one minute.

  2. Add the tomato, broccoli, garlic and spices cook for one more minute.

  3. Add the beef and soy sauce and cook for a further minute.

  4. Serve immediately.
