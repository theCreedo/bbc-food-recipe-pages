Spinach-filled mushrooms with grated cheddar

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 100g/3½oz spinach , washed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 large flat mushrooms
  • 55g/2oz cheddar , grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Heat one tablespoon of the olive oil in a frying pan and fry the garlic for one minute.

  3. Add the spinach and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until just wilted. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. Place the mushrooms stalk-side up on a baking sheet and drizzle each one with a tablespoon of olive oil.

  5. Divide the spinach between the two mushrooms and top with the grated cheese.

  6. Bake in the oven for 3-4 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden-brown.

  7. To serve, place each mushroom onto a small serving plate.
