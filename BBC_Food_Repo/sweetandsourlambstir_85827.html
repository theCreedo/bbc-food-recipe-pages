Sweet and sour lamb stir fry

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 150g/5½oz lamb chump chop, trimmed of fat, sliced
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives , to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat and fry the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes, until softened.

  2. Add the lamb slices and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes.

  3. Add the chilli flakes, sesame oil, soy sauce, tomato ketchup, sesame seeds and honey. Stir-fry until the lamb is completely cooked through, then season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  4. To serve, place the stir fry onto a plate and sprinkle with chives.
