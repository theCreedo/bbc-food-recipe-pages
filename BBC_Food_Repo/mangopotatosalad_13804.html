Mango potato salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Sarah Lynch
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2¼lb small new potatoes , scrubbed
  • 1 medium onion , finely chopped
  • mayonnaise
  • mango chutney (approximately half the amount of mayonnaise)
  • handful fresh mint , chopped
  • handful fresh parsley , chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Cook the potatoes in a pan of boiling salted water until tender, drain and leave to cool.

  2. In a large serving dish mix together the onion, mayonnaise, chutney and half the mint and half of the parsley. Mix well and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Once the potatoes are cool enough to handle, slice them in half. Depending on the size of potatoes, I like to cut some into halves and some into quarters rather than all uniform size.

  4. Mix the potatoes into the mayonnaise and onion mix. Stir well and sprinkle over the remaining mint and parsley.

  5. Check for seasoning and get stuck in!
