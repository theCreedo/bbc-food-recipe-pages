Oriental marinade

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 175ml/6fl oz

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp groundnut oil
  • 4 tbsp pale sherry or rice wine
  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic , crushed
  • 2.5cm fresh ginger , finely grated
  • freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Combine all ingredients. Cover the meat, poultry, fish or shellfish and leave for 2-3 hours.
