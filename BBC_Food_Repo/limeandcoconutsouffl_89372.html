Lime and coconut soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 200ml/7fl oz coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 lime, zest only
  • 75g/2¾oz butter , plus extra for preparing the ramekins
  • 3 free-range eggs , separated
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar , plus extra for dusting the ramekins

To serve

  • icing sugar , for dusting

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Brush two ramekin dishes with a little melted butter. Add a small amount of sugar to each, turn them to coat the sides and bottom, shaking out any excess.

  3. Heat the coconut milk, flour, cornflour, lime zest and butter in a saucepan over a medium heat for 5-6 minutes, or until thickened slightly.

  4. Put the egg yolks into a bowl and pour the coconut milk mixture over them, whisking continuously until well combined.

  5. In a separate, clean bowl, whisk the egg whites with the sugar until it forms soft peaks when the whisk is removed.

  6. Carefully fold the coconut mixture into the egg white mixture.

  7. Pour the soufflé mixture into the ramekins, level off the tops with a palette knife and place onto a baking tray. Transfer to the oven for 6-8 minutes, or until risen and golden-brown.

  8. To serve, dust with icing sugar using a sieve. Serve immediately.
