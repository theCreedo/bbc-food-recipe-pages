Pan-fried halibut on spiced tomatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pan-fried halibut

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g/3½oz halibut steak
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the spiced tomatoes

  • dash olive oil
  • 2 fresh tomatoes, cut in half
  • pinch paprika
  • pinch curry powder
  • pinch ground cinnamon
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. For the halibut, heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add the halibut, season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and fry for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

  2. For the spiced tomatoes, heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Place the tomatoes cut-side down into the pan and fry over a gentle heat for 2-3 minutes. Turn over and season with the spices, salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. To serve, place the spiced tomatoes onto a serving plate and top with the halibut.
