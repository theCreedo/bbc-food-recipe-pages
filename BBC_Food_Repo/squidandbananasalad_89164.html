Squid and banana salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Meera Syal
From A Taste of My Life

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp groundnut oil
  • 450g/1lb fresh squid , cleaned, cut into strips
  • 2-4 fresh chillies (green or red), chopped

For the citrus dressing

  • ½ orange , zest and juice only
  • ½ lemon , zest and juice only
  • 3 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

To serve

  • 3 bananas , peeled, chopped
  • 3 tbsp sesame seeds , toasted in a dry frying pan

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the squid and chillies. Fry for 1-2 minutes, until the squid turns opaque (no longer or it will become tough). Remove with slotted spoon and set aside.

  2. For the dressing, place all of the dressing ingredients into a bowl and mix well.

  3. To serve, place the squid mixture into a large bowl. Add the bananas, toasted sesame seeds and the dressing and stir well.
