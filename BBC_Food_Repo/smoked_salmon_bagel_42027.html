Smoked salmon bagel

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

The classic bagel with smoked salmon and cream cheese gets its healthy makeover. And it makes a great breakfast or lunch.

By Emily Angle

Ingredients

  • 1 wholemeal or 50/50 thin bagel , sliced in half, toasted
  • 15g/½oz light cream cheese
  • 35g/1oz smoked salmon , torn into pieces
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • 1-2 slices of tomato
  • 1 thin slice of red onion (optional)
  • freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Spread the cream cheese onto the toasted bagel, cover with the smoked salmon strips and squeeze over the lemon juice. Add the slices of tomato and red onion, then season to taste with freshly ground black pepper.
