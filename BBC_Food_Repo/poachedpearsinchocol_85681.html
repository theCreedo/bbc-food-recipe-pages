Poached pears in chocolate sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Miranda Richardson
From A Taste of My Life

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-sized pears , peeled but stalks left on
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 450ml/16fl oz water
  • 200g/7oz caster sugar

For the chocolate sauce

  • 150ml/5oz double cream
  • 200g/7oz dark chocolate , roughly chopped

Method

  1. Brush the peeled pears with a little lemon juice.

  2. Heat the water to a simmer in a large pan and add the sugar. Leave the sugar and water to simmer until it has slightly thickened, before adding the pears to the pan.

  3. Cover the pan and poach the pears for 25 minutes.

  4. Carefully remove the pears from the poaching liquid and transfer to six individual serving plates.

  5. Pour the double cream into a clean pan and heat gently, without boiling.

  6. Take the pan off the heat and add the chopped chocolate, stirring to make a smooth sauce.

  7. To serve, pour the chocolate sauce over the pears.
