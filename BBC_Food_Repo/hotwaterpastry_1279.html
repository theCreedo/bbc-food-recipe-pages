Hotwater pastry

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 6-8

Ingredients

  • 225g/8oz plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 egg , beaten
  • 100ml/3½fl oz water
  • 80g/3oz lard

Method

  1. Put the flour, salt and egg into a bowl and roughly mix together.

  2. Put the water and lard in a small saucepan and heat together until it reaches boiling point.

  3. Stir into the flour mixture and bring into a ball. Wrap in clingfilm and rest at room temperature for 5 minutes before moulding into your pie dish or mould.

  4. The pastry will harden as it cools.
