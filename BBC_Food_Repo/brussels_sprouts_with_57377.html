Brussels sprouts with toasted almonds and bacon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

This is a great way to serve sprouts. It would make a lovely side dish for the Christmas Day turkey or with cold cuts on Boxing Day.

By Andi Oliver
From Christmas Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 4 slices smoked or unsmoked streaky bacon , roughly chopped
  • 1 banana shallot , thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 250g/9oz Brussels sprouts , finely shredded
  • 30g/1oz toasted almonds , roughly chopped

Method

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and fry the bacon until crisp. Transfer to a plate lined with kitchen paper to drain.

  2. Add the shallot and garlic to the pan and cook for 2 minutes, until soft. Add the sprouts and cook for 3-4 minutes, until wilted, stirring occasionally.

  3. Return the bacon to the pan, add the almonds and cook through for 2 minutes. Serve immediately.
