Grilled pineapple

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian

Worth keeping the barbecue hot for! Makes a delicious change from marshmallows.

By The Hairy Bikers
From Kitchen Garden Live with the Hairy Bikers

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple , peeled, cored and cut into 8 wedges
  • 1 lime , juice only
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp dark rum
  • 4 tbsp plain yoghurt or crème fraiche

Method

  1. Heat a barbecue or griddle pan to a high heat.

  2. Place the pineapple in a large bowl. Add the lime juice, maple syrup, ginger and rum and mix well to combine.

  3. Cook the pineapple for 2–3 minutes on each side, or until lightly charred.

  4. Serve with the yoghurt or crème fraiche.
