Pimm’s

Preparation time

1-2 hours

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

Vegetarian

A classic summer cocktail. Perfect for a balmy garden party.

Ingredients

  • 750ml/26fl oz lemonade
  • 250ml/9fl oz Pimm’s
  • 1 cucumber , roughly chopped
  • 200g/7oz strawberries , hulled and halved
  • handful borage leaves and flowers
  • handful mint leaves
  • handful lemon balm
  • 1 lemon , sliced
  • 1 orange , sliced
  • ice

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients, except the ice, in a large bowl and mix. Allow to stand for 1 hour before serving.

  2. Pour into serving glasses, topped with ice, for a delicious summer drink.
