Healthy oven chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 3-4

Dietary

Vegetarian

Cooking at a high temperature gives you crisp, crunchy chips using just a teaspoon of oil.

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2lb 2oz floury potatoes , such as King Edward, peeled and cut into thick chips
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 240C/220C Fan/Gas 9.

  2. Boil the chips in lightly salted water for 4 minutes, then drain well in a colander. When cool enough to handle, tip them onto a baking tray and drizzle over the oil. Mix to coat the chips in the oil, then arrange them in a single layer and season with salt and pepper.

  3. Bake for 20 minutes. Turn the chips, season again, then bake for 15 minutes more.
