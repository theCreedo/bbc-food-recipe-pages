Pea and mint soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ red onion , peeled and diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • ½ pt vegetable stock
  • handful fresh mint
  • handful fresh peas
  • drizzle cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan and sauté the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes, to soften.

  2. Pour in the stock and bring to the boil. Add the mint and peas. Reduce the heat and simmer gently for 8-10 minutes.

  3. Transfer the mixture to a liquidiser and blend until smooth.

  4. Return to the pan to warm through and add the cream, salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  5. Ladle into soup bowls to serve.
