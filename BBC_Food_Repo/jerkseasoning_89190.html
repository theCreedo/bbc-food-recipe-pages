Jerk seasoning

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Patrick Williams
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch fresh thyme , leaves only
  • 2 cinnamon sticks, roughly crushed
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds , crushed
  • 2 tbsp black peppercorns , crushed
  • 1 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 tsp allspice , crushed
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 4 Scotch Bonnet chilli es, seeds removed
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh ginger
  • 2 limes, zest of 1 lime, juice of 2
  • 140ml/¼ pint olive oil

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients in a blender or food processor and blend to make a smooth paste.

  2. Use the paste as a seasoning for chicken, pork or fish. It will keep for two weeks if covered and refrigerated.
