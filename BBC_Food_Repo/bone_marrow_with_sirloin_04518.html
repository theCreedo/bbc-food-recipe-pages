Bone marrow with sirloin steak, bordelaise sauce and sautéed sprouts with chestnuts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Bone marrow is wonderfully rich and perfect served alongside sirloin steak.

By James Martin
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the bone marrow and sirloin steak

  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 shallots , halved and skin left on
  • 5 x 2.5cm/1in cut bone marrow , cleaned
  • 4 sirloin steaks

For the bordelaise sauce

  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter
  • 1 shallot , finely chopped
  • 2 sprigs thyme , leaves picked
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 5 black pepper corns
  • 200ml/7fl oz red wine
  • 300ml/10fl oz veal or beef stock , warmed
  • pinch sugar

For the sauté sprouts with chestnuts

  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter
  • 250g/9oz Brussels sprouts , thinly sliced
  • 70g/2½oz cooked chestnuts , chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4. Heat 4 tablespoons of oil in a frying pan, add the shallots and fry until golden. Transfer to a roasting tray and roast for 15-20 minutes.

  2. Add the bone marrow to the roasting tray and return to the oven for 6 minutes, or until the marrow is just cooked, loose but not completely melted. Leave to rest in a warm place.

  3. Heat the remaining oil in the frying pan. Add the steaks and cook each side for 2-3 minutes, depending on the thickness of the steak and how you like it cooked. Leave to rest.

  4. For the bordelaise sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the shallot and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the thyme, bay leaf and peppercorns and cook for 3 minutes.

  5. Add the wine and cook until the volume of liquid has reduced by half. Pour in the stock and reduce by half again. Season with a little sugar, to taste. Add the bone marrow from one of the roasted pieces, whisk and set aside. Keep warm.

  6. For the saute sprouts, heat a sauté pan. Add the sprouts and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add the chestnuts and parsley and cook for a further 2 minutes.

  7. Serve the steak with the roasted shallots and bone marrow and the sprouts, with the sauce poured over.
