Pasta with onions, tomato, chilli and olive oil

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , sliced
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 baby plum tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 150g/5½oz shell pasta , cooked according to packet instructions
  • 1 handful fresh chervil , to garnish

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and gently fry the onion for 3-4 minutes, or until softened. Stir in the chilli flakes and the plum tomatoes and cook for a further 3-4 minutes.

  2. Stir in the chopped parsley, then add the cooked pasta to the sauce and stir gently to combine.

  3. To serve, pile the pasta onto a serving plate and garnish with the fresh chervil.
