Mom’s meatloaf

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4-6

Meatloaf is a classic of American home cooking. This is great served hot or cold with a tangy potato salad.

By Nancy Davis
From The Hairy Bikers: Mums Know Best

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1kg/2lb 4oz beef mince
  • 1 large red onion , chopped
  • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 2 tbsp brown sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 70g/2½oz parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Place the bread in a food processor and pulse to make breadcrumbs.

  3. Mix the breadcrumbs with the remaining ingredients in a large bowl until the mixture pulls away from the sides of the bowl and forms a ball.

  4. Form the mixture into a loaf shape and place in an ovenproof dish or loaf tin. Bake in the oven for 50–60 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
