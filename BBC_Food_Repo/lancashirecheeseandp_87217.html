Lancashire cheese and prune filo parcels

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Makes 12

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 4 sheets ready-made filo pastry
  • 55g/2oz butter , melted
  • 100g/3½oz Lancashire cheese, grated
  • 12 prunes , stones removed

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Cut each sheet of filo pastry into three long strips and brush with melted butter.

  3. Place a small spoonful of cheese at one end of each strip and top with a prune.

  4. Fold one corner of the pastry over the filling to the opposite side to make a triangle. Continue folding up until all the pastry is used up and brush all over with butter. Repeat to make 12 parcels.

  5. Place onto a baking sheet and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until golden-brown and crisp.

  6. To serve, place onto a serving plate.
