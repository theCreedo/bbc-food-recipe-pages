Bunny burgers with chunky chips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 2

This is a great recipe if you haven't tried rabbit before - juicy rabbit burgers with homemade chips.

By The Barber Family
From The Hairy Bikers' Cook Off

Ingredients

For the chips

  • 2-3 medium sized baking potatoes , peeled, cut into chips
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

For the burgers

  • 1 onion , finely chopped
  • 350g/12oz minced rabbit
  • 100g/4oz minced pork belly
  • 4 fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/gas 7.

  2. For the chips, bring a large pan of salted water to the boil, add the potatoes and boil for five minutes. Drain, then toss in the olive oil, season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper and spread out on a baking tray.

  3. Transfer to the oven for about 20 minutes, or until golden-brown and cooked through.

  4. For the burgers, mix together all of the burger ingredients apart from the olive oil. Season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, divide the mixture in half and shape into two large burgers.

  5. Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium heat and fry the burgers for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown and cooked through.

  6. Serve the burger with the chips and tomato ketchup.
