Mint sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6-8

Dietary

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 25g/1oz fresh mint , chopped
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp hot water
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar

Method

  1. Mix all the ingredients together and stand for 20 minutes to allow the mint flavour to develop. The classic mint sauce to serve with roast lamb.
