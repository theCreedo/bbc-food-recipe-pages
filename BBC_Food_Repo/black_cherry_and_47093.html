Black cherry and hazelnut jam

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes about 600ml/20fl oz (about 2 jam jars)

Dietary

Vegetarian

Adding grated Bramley apples to this cherry jam recipe gives a firmer consistency and a delicate flavour.

By Dan Lepard

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz fresh cherries
  • 200g/7oz (about 2) Bramley apples
  • 350g/12oz granulated or caster sugar
  • 1 small lemon , juice only
  • 50g/2oz chopped, roasted hazelnuts

Method

  1. Put the cherries (either pitted, whole or halved) into a saucepan and cover with 500ml/18fl oz water. Bring to the boil, then cook until the cherries are very tender and the water almost evaporated.

  2. Peel and grate the Bramley apples, minus the core and pips, and add the grated apple to the pan. Boil again until the apple threads are very soft and falling apart and the liquid has almost disappeared.

  3. Add the sugar and lemon juice, bring to the boil and cook until the preserve reaches 105C/220C on a sugar thermometer. Stir in the hazelnuts towards the end of cooking.

  4. Leave to cool for a few minutes, then pour into hot clean jars and seal immediately.

  5. Leave the jam until the following day before eating, and after opening keep in the refrigerator.
