Cucumber Margarita

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Wayne Collins
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 2.5cm/1in piece cucumber , peeled, seeds removed, diced, plus one slice cucumber , to garnish
  • 20ml/¾fl oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tsp elderflower cordial
  • 50ml/2fl oz silver tequila
  • 15ml/½fl oz triple sec
  • 1 scoop ice

Method

  1. Place the diced cucumber into a cocktail shaker and mash (muddle) with the end of a clean rolling pin.

  2. Add the lime juice, elderflower cordial, silver tequila, triple sec and a scoop of ice. Shake well.

  3. To serve, strain the mixture into a coupette glass. Garnish with the slice of fresh cucumber.
