Hard-boiled eggs with mustard mayonnaise

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 10

Dietary

Vegetarian

Pack these boiled eggs and homemade mayonnaise for a nostalgic picnic.

By Rick Stein

Ingredients

  • 10-12 eggs

For the mustard mayonnaise

  • 1 tbsp English mustard
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • A good pinch of salt
  • A few turns of white pepper mill
  • 300ml/½ pint sunflower oil

Method

  1. Hardboil the eggs for 8 minutes.

  2. Drain, cover with cold water and leave to cool.

  3. To make the mustard mayonnaise, mix the mustard, egg yolks, white wine vinegar together in a bowl.

  4. Very gradually whisk in the oil to make a thick and creamy mayonnaise.

  5. Spoon into a pot, cover and chill until needed. Serve with the peeled hard boiled eggs.
