Steamed sole and baby leeks with mustard mash

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the steamed sole

  • 150g/5½oz sole fillet, halved lengthways
  • 2 baby leeks , halved lengthways
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the mustard mash

  • 200g/7oz new potatoes , halved
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream

To serve

  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Method

  1. For the steamed sole, place the sole fillet strips onto a work surface and place the baby leeks across the fish. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper and roll up the baby leeks in the sole fillet strips. Carefully place the fish rolls into a steamer over a pan of boiling water and steam for 5-7 minutes, or until cooked through.

  2. For the mustard mash, boil the new potato halves until tender and then drain. Return to the pan, add the mustard and cream and mash well with a potato masher.

  3. Place the steamed sole onto a serving plate with the mashed potatoes. Garnish with chopped dill and parsley and serve immediately.
