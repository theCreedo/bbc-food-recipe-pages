Smoked salmon, spring onion and cream cheese roulade

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 2-4

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz smoked salmon slices
  • 100g/3½oz cream cheese
  • 3 spring onions , very finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • ¼ tsp cayenne paper
  • fresh dill sprigs, to garnish

Method

  1. Place a sheet of cling film on a work surface. Lay the smoked salmon slices in a rectangle about 20cm x 10cm/8in x 4in, overlapping the slices slightly so that there are no gaps.

  2. Spread the cream cheese over the smoked salmon and sprinkle with the sliced spring onions, chopped dill and cayenne pepper.

  3. Roll the roulade up lengthways and twist the cling film at the ends, so it looks like a Christmas cracker. Chill in the fridge for an hour, if possible.

  4. Slice the roulade into 2cm/¾in slices and arrange on a serving platter. Garnish with dill sprigs and serve.
