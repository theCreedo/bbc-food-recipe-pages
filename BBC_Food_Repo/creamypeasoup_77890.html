Creamy pea soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the creamy pea soup

  • ½ red onion , diced
  • 25g/1oz butter
  • 200g/7oz peas (tinned or frozen, thawed)
  • 300ml chicken or vegetable stock
  • 100ml double cream
  • 2 tbsp parsley , chopped

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan. When foaming add the onions and sweat for three minutes.

  2. Add the peas and cook for a further four minutes.

  3. Add the stock and cream and cook for one minute.

  4. Blend the soup in a food processor and serve in a large bowl garnished with the chopped parsley.
