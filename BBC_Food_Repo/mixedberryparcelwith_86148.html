Mixed berry parcel with berry coulis

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Alex Mackay
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the mixed berry parcel

  • 10cm/4in square ready-rolled puff pastry
  • icing sugar , to dust
  • 1 tbsp frozen berries, defrosted
  • 1 free-range egg , lightly beaten

For the coulis

  • 1 tbsp icing sugar
  • 100g/3½oz frozen berries, defrosted, plus extra to serve
  • fresh mint leaves, to garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the mixed berry parcel, dust the pastry with icing sugar and spoon the berries into the centre.

  3. Brush the edges of the pastry with the beaten egg and fold the corners up into the centre. Pinch the edges together to seal the filling inside the pastry.

  4. Place the parcel onto a non-stick baking sheet, transfer to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until slightly risen and golden-brown.

  5. For the coulis, place the icing sugar and berries into a food processor and blend until smooth. Strain the mixture through a sieve to remove the pips.

  6. To serve, pour a pool of coulis onto a serving plate and top with the mixed berry parcel. Garnish with berries and fresh mint.
