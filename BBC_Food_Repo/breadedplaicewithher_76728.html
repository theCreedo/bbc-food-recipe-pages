Breaded plaice with herbed rice salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Tony Tobin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 plaice fillets
  • 30g/1oz plain flour
  • 1 egg , beaten
  • 30g/1oz white breadcrumbs
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil

For the rice salad

  • 110g/4oz basmati rice , cooked
  • ½ red pepper , diced
  • 1 orange , juice only
  • 3 tbsp parsley , chopped
  • 3 tbsp coriander , chopped

Method

  1. Coat the plaice fillets in flour, then in the beaten egg and finally in the breadcrumbs.

  2. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the fillets for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

  3. For the salad, mix the rice, pepper, orange juice and fresh herbs into a large bowl.

  4. Spoon the rice onto a serving plate and serve the breaded fillets alongside.
