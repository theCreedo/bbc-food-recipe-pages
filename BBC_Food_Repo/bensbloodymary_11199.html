Non-alcoholic Bloody Mary

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 litre jug

Dietary

Vegetarian

Make a jug of spiky, savoury non-alcoholic Bloody Mary for a weekend brunch that won't make you feel fuzzy.

By Ben O'Donoghue
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 litre/1¾ pint tomato juice
  • 1 bunch celery leaves (washed and picked from ½ a bunch of celery )
  • dash Tabasco sauce (to taste)
  • dash creamed horseradish
  • dash HP sauce (to taste)
  • 2 limes , juice only
  • crushed ice (optional)

Method

  1. Put everything in a big jug and mix

  2. Add a handful of crushed ice if you like but Ben has his without!
