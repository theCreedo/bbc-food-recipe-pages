Caramelised apple with red wine and mint sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the caramelised apple

  • 15g/½oz butter
  • 1 cooking apple , sliced thinly
  • 40g/1½oz soft brown sugar
  • pinch salt
  • 175ml/6fl oz red wine
  • 1 orange , juice only

For the mint sauce

  • 2 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint

To serve

  • 1 fresh mint sprig
  • icing sugar , to sprinkle

Method

  1. For the caramelised apple, heat the butter in a frying pan and fry the apple slices until coloured on both sides. Remove the apple slices and set aside.

  2. Place the brown sugar, salt, red wine and orange juice into the same pan and heat until the sugar is dissolved and the liquid has reduced slightly to a syrupy sauce. Return the apple slices to the pan and warm through in the sauce.

  3. For the mint sauce, combine the ingredients in a bowl and stir well.

  4. To serve, arrange the apple slices in a spiral on a plate and spoon the red wine syrup over the top. Drizzle over the mint sauce, garnish with a sprig of mint and sprinkle with icing sugar.
