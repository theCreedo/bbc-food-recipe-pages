Nectarine and ginger tarte tatin

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pastry

  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 20g/¾oz butter
  • pinch salt
  • ½ free-range egg

For the fruit

  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 5cm/2in piece of galangal or ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 nectarines , halved and stones removed
  • icing sugar , to dust
  • 100ml/3½fl oz double cream , whipped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place all the pastry ingredients together in a food processor and blend until a dough forms. Wrap the dough in cling film and transfer to the fridge to chill for 10 minutes.

  3. For the fruit, melt butter in a small ovenproof frying pan over a medium heat. Add the sugar and stir until dissolved.

  4. Add the galangal or ginger to the frying pan and cook for two minutes.

  5. Add the nectarines to the frying pan, with the cut side facing down. Cook for five fminutes until they become soft and brown underneath.

  6. Roll out the pastry on a lightly floured surface. Cut a disc to match the diameter of the frying pan and use to cover the nectarines. Pinch the edges to seal and transfer the frying pan to the oven. Bake for 12 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  7. To serve, turn upside down over a serving plate and tip out. Dust with icing sugar and serve with the whipped cream.
