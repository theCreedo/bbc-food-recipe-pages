Simple peanut sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

By Ian Pengelley
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 500g/1lb 1oz crushed roasted peanuts
  • 300g/10½oz sweetened rice vinegar , known as sushi vinegar
  • 500g/1lb 1oz caster sugar
  • 1½ tbsp Kaypa or red curry paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce or light soy sauce

Method

  1. Blend all the ingredients together in the blender until smooth, leave in fridge to set.

  2. The taste should be sweet, nutty and spicy. It can be served with a variety of dishes including chicken, pork or prawn satay.
