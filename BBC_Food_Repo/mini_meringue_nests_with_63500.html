Mini meringue nests with pistachios and raspberries

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

over 2 hours

Serves

Makes 12

Dietary

Vegetarian

These dainty little mouthfuls are a clever twist on a classic pavlova and make perfect petit fours.

By Joanne Wheatley
From The Great British Bake Off

Ingredients

  • 3 free-range egg whites
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 150g/5½oz caster sugar
  • 115g/4oz unsalted pistachios , blended to a fine powder
  • 115g/4oz white chocolate with dried raspberries, melted
  • 142ml/¼ pint double cream
  • 115g/4oz white chocolate , shaved with a knife
  • 12 fresh raspberries
  • 12 small mint leaves

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 110C/225F/Gas ¼. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper.

  2. Whisk the egg whites and lemon juice in a bowl until they form stiff peaks, then fold in the sugar and pistachios. Spoon the mixture into a piping bag fitted with a plain nozzle and pipe small nests onto the baking tray. Bake in the oven for two hours, lthen remove from the oven and set aside to cool completely. Carefully remove the meringues from the paper.

  3. Spoon the melted chocolate into the meringue nests. Whip the cream in a bowl until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Spoon the cream into a piping bag fitted with a small nozzle and pipe into the middle of the nests.

  4. Shave the white chocolate over the nests and decorate each one with a raspberry and a mint leaf.
