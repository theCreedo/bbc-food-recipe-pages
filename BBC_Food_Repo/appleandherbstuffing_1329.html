Apple and herb stuffing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

4

Dietary

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 eating apple , cored and finely chopped
  • 80g/3oz fresh white breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp fresh herbs, parsley, thyme, sage or tarragon, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 egg , beaten

Method

  1. Mix together the apple, breadcrumbs and herbs and season well.

  2. Add enough of the egg to bind all together and use to stuff meat or poultry or roll into individual stuffing balls.

  3. If making stuffing balls, bake in the oven with the meat for 30 minutes.
