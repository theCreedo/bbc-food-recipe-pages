Gammon, pea and orange salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz gammon steak
  • ¼ onion , sliced
  • 150g/5oz fresh peas
  • 1 orange , segmented
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tbsp oil

Method

  1. Place the gammon into a saucepan. Cover with water and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for ten minutes, or until the gammon is completely cooked through.

  2. In a bowl, mix together the onion, peas, orange segments, mint and oil.

  3. Place the salad on a serving plate. Cut the gammon into strips and scatter over the salad.
