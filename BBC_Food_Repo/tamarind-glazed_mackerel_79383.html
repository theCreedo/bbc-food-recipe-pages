Tamarind-glazed mackerel with lemongrass noodles

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 6-8

Give fresh mackerel fillets an oriental twist with James Martin's speedy noodle dish.

By James Martin
From Saturday Kitchen Best Bites

Ingredients

For the mackerel

  • 4 mackerel , cleaned and filleted
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger , grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp clear honey

For the lemongrass noodles

  • 450g/1lb egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 stem lemongrass , finely sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 5cm/2in piece ginger , peeled and finely diced
  • 2 long green chillies , finely diced
  • 4 spring onions , sliced
  • ½ Chinese leaf, finely shredded
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 limes , juice only
  • 3 tbsp fresh coriander , roughly chopped

Method

  1. For the mackerel, place the mackerel fillets onto a grill pan or flat tray. Mix the remaining ingredients together and brush over both sides of the fillets. Set aside to marinade for 30 minutes.

  2. To cook the mackerel, heat the grill to its highest setting. Grill the fillets for two minutes on each side, basting with the marinade as you turn them.

  3. For the lemongrass noodles, cook the egg noodles in a large pan of boiling water according to the packet instructions then drain, refresh in iced water and drain again thoroughly. Toss the noodles with half of the olive oil and set aside.

  4. Heat a frying pan until medium hot, add the remaining olive oil, lemongrass, garlic, ginger, chilli and spring onions and cook gently for 1-2 minutes, or until the vegetables have just softened. Mix the noodles into the vegetables and stir in the Chinese leaf.

  5. Whisk the oyster and soy sauces with the sesame oil and lime juice, then mix into the noodles and season to taste with salt.

  6. To serve, pile the noodles onto each plate, top with the grilled mackerel and serve garnished with chopped coriander.
