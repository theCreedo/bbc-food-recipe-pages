Mini chocolate puddings with glazed satsumas

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the puddings

  • 150g/5½oz unsalted butter , plus extra for greasing
  • 150g/5½oz self-raising flour
  • 150g/5½oz brown sugar
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

For the satsumas

  • 2 satsumas , peeled and sliced in half sideways
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar

For the sauce

  • 150ml/5fl oz milk
  • 150ml/5fl oz double cream
  • 50g/1½oz brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter

Method

  1. Preheat a baking sheet in the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the pudding, place all the pudding ingredients into the bowl of a food processor and blend until a smooth mixture is formed.

  3. Spoon the pudding mixture into two greased ramekins.

  4. Place the ramekins onto the hot baking tray and bake in the oven for 12 minutes, or until cooked through.

  5. For the satsumas, dust the satsumas with icing sugar and place them onto a baking tray and bake for ten minutes.

  6. For the sauce, place all the sauce ingredients apart from the butter into a saucepan and heat, while stirring, until the ingredients have melted and combined to form a smooth mixture. Add the butter and stir until it has melted and combined with the sauce.

  7. To serve, remove the puddings from the ramekins and place them onto clean plates. Spoon some of the sauce over the top of the puddings and garnish with the satsumas.
