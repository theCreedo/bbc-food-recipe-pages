Salmon and roasted garlic cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Sweet roasted garlic goes brilliantly with cream and salmon. Buy the freshest salmon you can find for this dish.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel Slater's 12 Tastes of Christmas

Ingredients

  • 1 head of garlic
  • 100g/3½oz salmon , sliced
  • 150ml/5fl oz double cream
  • 20g/1oz dill , chopped
  • 4 crispbreads

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Bake the whole head of garlic for 30-45 minutes or until the cloves are tender.

  3. Pull the head apart and squeeze the contents of each clove, which will now be like a paste, into a food processor.

  4. Pour in the double cream and half the dill, and pulse gently to combine.

  5. Place the salmon onto the crispbreads. Spoon over the cream and scatter over the remaining dill.
