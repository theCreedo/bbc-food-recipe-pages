Pork and date skewers with couscous salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1-2

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pork and date skewers

  • olive oil , for brushing
  • 75g/3oz pork fillet , cut into 5 cubes
  • 5 dates , stones removed
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds

For the couscous salad</stong>

  • 125g/4½oz couscous
  • 125ml/4fl oz hot vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • handful fresh parsley and fresh basil, chopped
  • 3 dates , stones removed, chopped
  • ½ yellow plum , chopped
  • 50g/1¾oz goats' cheese , crumbled
  • fresh chives , to garnish

Method

  1. Lightly oil a griddle pan and place over a high heat. Thread the pork cubes and dates onto a skewer, then place the skewer onto the pan and cook until golden-brown on all sides. 2. Drizzle the soy sauce and honey and sprinkle the sesame seeds over the pork and dates and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until completely cooked through.

  2. Meanwhile, for the couscous salad, place the couscous into a bowl and pour over enough hot vegetable stock to cover the couscous. Cover the bowl with cling film and leave to stand for 4-5 minutes.

  3. Remove the cling film, separate the grains of couscous with a fork, then add all of the remaining ingredients to the bowl and mix well.

  4. Lightly oil a 6cm/2½in chefs' ring or a small glass bowl and place onto a serving plate. Spoon the couscous salad into the ring or bowl, then remove the ring or turn the bowl out onto the plate and carefully lift off.

  5. To serve, place the skewer on top of the couscous salad and garnish with chives.
