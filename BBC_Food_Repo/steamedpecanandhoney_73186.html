Steamed pecan and honey pudding

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 3-4

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 115g/4oz sugar
  • 115g/4oz butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 115g/4oz self-raising flour
  • 1 orange , zest only
  • 1 lemon , zest only
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp pecan nuts
  • whipped cream , to serve
  • 3 tbsp apple sauce , to serve

Method

  1. Beat the sugar and butter together.

  2. Add the eggs and fold in the flour.

  3. Add the orange and lemon zest.

  4. Grease a small, heat-resistant glass pudding bowl, then drizzle the honey inside the bowl.

  5. Pour the pudding mixture into the bowl.

  6. Cover the bowl with cling film and microwave the bowl on high for 4-5 minutes, or until the sponge is cooked.

  7. Remove sponge from microwave and discard the cling film.

  8. Tip the sponge out onto a flat plate.

  9. Top sponge with combined whipped cream and apple sauce and the nuts and serve while still hot.
