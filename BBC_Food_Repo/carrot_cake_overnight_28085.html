Carrot cake overnight oats

Preparation time

overnight

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian

Rather like having cake for breakfast, but a lot better for you!

By Emily Angle

Ingredients

  • 40g/1½oz porridge oats or rolled oats
  • 40g/1½oz carrot , finely grated
  • ½ apple , finely grated
  • small handful raisins
  • ½ tsp mixed spice
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 150ml/5fl oz milk or soya milk

Method

  1. Place all the ingredients in a jar, stir, put the lid on and store in the fridge overnight.
