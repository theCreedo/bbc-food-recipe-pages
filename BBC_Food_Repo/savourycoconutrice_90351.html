Savoury coconut rice

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Maria Elia
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½ oz basmati rice
  • 200ml/7fl oz water
  • 200ml/7fl oz coconut milk
  • 1 handful pak choi stalks, chopped
  • ½ red chilli , chopped
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh coriander

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients except the coriander in a saucepan and bring to the boil. Simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the rice is tender, adding more water if necessary.

  2. Stir in the coriander and spoon into bowls to serve.
