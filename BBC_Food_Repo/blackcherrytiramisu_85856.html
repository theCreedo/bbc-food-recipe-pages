Black cherry tiramisu

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 5 free-range egg yolks
  • 100g/3½oz sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod , split, seeds scraped out
  • 500g/1lb 1½oz mascarpone
  • 200ml/7fl oz espresso-strength coffee
  • 100ml/3½fl oz Marsala wine
  • 24 ready-made sponge finger biscuits
  • 225g/8oz black cherries from a can or jar, drained slightly
  • cocoa powder , to dust

Method

  1. Place the eggs yolks, sugar and vanilla seeds into a bowl and whisk together until pale and light.

  2. Add the mascarpone and whisk to combine well.

  3. Place the coffee and Marsala into a clean bowl.

  4. Dip a sponge finger into the coffee mixture and press into the bottom of a tumbler or sundae glass. Repeat this process, using four sponge fingers in total, to line the bottom of the glass.

  5. Spoon some black cherries into the glass over the sponge fingers.

  6. Add a layer of the mascarpone mixture to cover the cherries.

  7. Repeat with a layer of black cherries and a layer of mascarpone mixture to fill the glass.

  8. Repeat this process to make five more desserts.

  9. To serve, smooth over the top of each tiramisu and dust each with cocoa powder.
