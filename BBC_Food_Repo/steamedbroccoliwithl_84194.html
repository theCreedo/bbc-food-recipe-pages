Steamed broccoli with lemon

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of broccoli , steamed
  • 1 lemon , zest and juice only
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Mix the steamed broccoli, lemon zest, lemon juice and oil together in a bowl.

  2. To serve, place the broccoli on a plate.
