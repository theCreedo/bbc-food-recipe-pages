Prawn and almond soup

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 5 tbsp white wine
  • 50g/1¾oz diced potato
  • 50g/1¾oz ground almonds
  • 400ml/14½fl oz chicken stock
  • 4 large prawns shell on, butterflied
  • 2 large handfuls baby spinach

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and fry the onions until soft.

  2. Add the wine and reduce for two minutes.

  3. Add the potato, almonds and stock cook for five minutes.

  4. Finish by adding the prawns and cook for three minutes and stir through the spinach.

  5. Serve.
