Instant banana ice cream

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian

This easy banana ice cream makes a great healthy dessert for children.

By James Martin
From Sweet Baby James

Ingredients

  • 4 very ripe bananas , peeled, cut into chunks
  • ¼ tsp vanilla essence
  • 3-4 tbsp sugar , honey or sweetener, to taste
  • 150ml/5fl oz buttermilk or 150g/5½oz Greek-style yoghurt

Method

  1. Spread the banana chunks out on a tray and transfer to the freezer. Leave the banana in the freezer until frozen all the way through (about one hour).

  2. Place the frozen banana chunks into a food processor. Add the vanilla, sugar, honey or sweetener and half of the buttermilk (or yoghurt). Turn on the processor and blend for a few moments. Then, while the processor is still running, pour in the remaining buttermilk in a thin, steady stream (if you are using yoghurt, just tip it all in and blend).

  3. Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Serve at once.
