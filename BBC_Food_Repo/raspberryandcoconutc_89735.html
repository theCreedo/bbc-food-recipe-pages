Raspberry and coconut cake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 12

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Simon Rimmer
From Something for the Weekend

Ingredients

  • 350g/12oz self-raising flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 350g/12oz unsalted butter , softened
  • 350g/12oz caster sugar
  • 6 free-range eggs
  • 200g/7oz desiccated coconut
  • 2 limes, juice and zest only
  • 350g/12oz raspberries , plus extra for decoration
  • 150g/5oz raspberry jam, thinned with a little hot water
  • 200g/7oz desiccated coconut , toasted

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/360F/Gas 4.

  2. Grease and line a 30cm/12in flat shallow cake tin with baking parchment.

  3. For the cake, beat the flour, baking powder, butter, sugar, eggs, coconut and lime juice together in a bowl (or use an electric mixer).

  4. Carefully fold in the raspberries.

  5. Pour the cake batter into the cake tin, then bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until risen and golden-brown. Test the cake with a metal skewer - if the skewer comes out clean, the cake is cooked through.

  6. Remove the cake from the oven and immediately spread the jam over the top.

  7. Allow the cake to cool then and sprinkle with the desiccated coconut and place the extra raspberries on top.

  8. To serve, cut the cake into squares.
