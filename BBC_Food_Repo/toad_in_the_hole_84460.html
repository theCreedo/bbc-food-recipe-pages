Tiny toad in the hole

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Home alone? This recipe for one can be whipped up in minutes. Serve with mash and green beans for an indulgent supper.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil , for greasing
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 100ml/3½fl oz milk
  • 50g/2oz plain flour
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • 2 chipolata sausages
  • 4 cherry tomatoes

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Grease a small ovenproof dish.

  2. Whisk the egg in a bowl, then whisk in the milk.

  3. Put the flour in a separate bowl, then slowly whisk in the egg mixture. Whisk in the mustard.

  4. Put the sausges and tomatoes into the dish and pour over the batter.

  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the batter is puffed up and golden-brown.
