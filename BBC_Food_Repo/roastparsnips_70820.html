Roast parsnips

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

The trick to these glorious golden roast parsnips is to add a pinch of cayenne pepper.

By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 400g/1lb parsnips
  • 100g/3½oz hot dripping
  • pinch cayenne pepper
  • 55g/2oz caster sugar

Method

  1. Leave small ones whole, and halve or quarter larger ones.

  2. Add to hot dripping having dipped them in a light dusting of cayenne pepper and caster sugar.

  3. Bake for 35-45 minutes.
