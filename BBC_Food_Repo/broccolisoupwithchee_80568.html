Broccoli soup with cheese scones

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the soup

  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 300ml/10½fl oz chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp fresh chopped chives
  • 1 broccoli head, broken up into florets
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil , to serve

For the scones

  • 100g/3½oz butter , diced
  • 200g/7oz self-raising flour
  • 1 free-range egg
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 25-50ml/1-2fl oz milk
  • 55g/2oz mature cheddar , grated

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the soup, place all of the ingredients, except the oil into a saucepan over a medium heat. Bring to the boil and reduce the heat to simmer for ten minutes, or until the broccoli is soft. Blend with a hand blender until smooth.

  3. Meanwhile, for the scones, place the butter and flour into a food processor. Blend until the mixture has the texture of fine breadcrumbs.

  4. Add the egg and salt and freshly ground black pepper and blend to combine. Continue to blend, gradually adding the milk, to make a soft, but not sticky, dough.

  5. Shape the dough into rounds with floured hands and place onto a baking sheet. Sprinkle over the cheddar and place in the oven to bake for ten minutes, or until risen and golden.

  6. To serve, ladle the soup into a bowl and drizzle over the oil and serve with a cheese scone.
