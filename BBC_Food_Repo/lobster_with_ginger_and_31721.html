Lobster with ginger and spring onions

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Lobster adds a touch of luxury to this super-quick stir fry.

By Angela Hartnett
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 x 225g/8oz lobsters
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2cm/1in fresh root ginger , peeled and finely chopped
  • 8 spring onions , trimmed and chopped
  • 4 pak choi , leaves separated
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • pinch white pepper
  • pinch caster sugar
  • 4 tbsp rice vinegar or Shoaxing rice wine
  • 100ml/3½fl oz chicken stock

Method

  1. Freeze the lobsters for 2 hours prior to cooking. Working quickly, take the first lobster, cut off the head using a sharp knife, then immediately cut the tail down the centre. Remove the flesh from the shell. Repeat with the second lobster. Cut each one into 6–8 pieces. Reserve and clean the shell for serving.

  2. Heat a wok or sauté pan over a high heat. Add half the sesame oil. Once hot, fry the lobster for 1 minute, then transfer it to a warm plate.

  3. Add the rest of the sesame oil to the pan. Fry the ginger, half the spring onions and the pak choi until softened. Return the lobster to the pan and stir in the remaining ingredients. Cover with a lid and simmer for 1 minute.

  4. Fill the shells with the meat. Divide between serving plates and garnish with the reserved spring onions.
