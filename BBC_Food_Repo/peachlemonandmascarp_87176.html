Peach, lemon and mascarpone trifle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons
  • 400g/14oz caster sugar
  • 200ml/7fl oz hot water, from a kettle
  • 4 sponge fingers, broken into pieces
  • 100g/3½oz mascarpone
  • 1 additional lemon , zest and juice
  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • 1 tbsp icing sugar , sifted
  • 1 peach , peeled and sliced

Method

  1. Cut one of the lemons into quarters and juice the other lemons. Place the quartered lemon and the lemon juice, sugar and hot water into a non-reactive saucepan and bring to the boil. Boil until the mixture is thick and syrupy then remove from the heat and allow to cool.

  2. Dip the sponge fingers into the sugar syrup and place in the base of a glass serving dish. (There will be sugar syrup left over to use on another occasion.).

  3. Mix the mascarpone, lemon zest and a squeeze of lemon juice from the aditional lemon, double cream and icing sugar together.

  4. Place a third of the peach slices on top of the sponge finger base and then top with half the cream mix.

  5. Place another third of the peach slices on top of the cream and cover with the rest of the cream mix.

  6. Decorate with the rest of the peaches and serve.
