Quinoa and bulgur wheat salad with lemon and pomegranate

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

Vegetarian

This zesty salad is packed full of flavour and goodness, perfect for popping in your lunchbox.

By Mary Berry
From Mary Berry Everyday

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz bulgur wheat
  • 150g/5½oz red quinoa , rinsed well and drained
  • 1 tbsp olive oil , for frying
  • 1 red onion , thinly sliced
  • 200g/7oz sun-blushed tomatoes in oil, drained and halved
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped flatleaf parsley
  • 2 tbsp finely snipped chives
  • 1 tbsp torn fresh basil leaves
  • 100g/3½oz feta cheese, broken into pieces
  • 50g/1¾oz pomegranate seeds, to garnish (optional)

For the dressing

  • 1 unwaxed lemon , finely grated zest and juice only
  • 2 tbsp pomegranate molasses
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Put the bulgar wheat and quinoa in a lidded saucepan and pour in 500ml/18fl oz water. Cover and bring to boil. Boil for 3–4 minutes – check the packet for instructions as cooking times can vary. Turn off the heat and leave for 20 minutes until cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Drain, if needed, and tip into a bowl.

  2. For the dressing, put the lemon zest and juice, pomegranate molasses, sugar and oil into a small bowl. Stir to combine.

  3. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a frying pan. Add the onion and fry for few minutes until nearly soft. Tip into the bowl with the bulgar wheat. Add the tomatoes and herbs. Season with salt and pepper and toss together. Add the dressing and mix.

  4. Gently mix in the feta and pomegranate seeds. Check the seasoning and add more salt and pepper, if required.
