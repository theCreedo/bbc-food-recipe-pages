Jansson’s parsnip temptation

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6

A Swedish inspired recipe. Root veg, anchovy paste and lashings of cream are baked into a truly irresistable side dish.

By Andi Oliver
From Christmas Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 x 30g tin anchovies in oil
  • 50g/1¾oz roasted garlic (see tip)
  • 2 medium parsnips , thinly sliced on mandoline
  • 2 medium potatoes , thinly sliced on mandoline
  • 1 medium white onion , thinly sliced on mandoline
  • 500ml/18fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 150C/130C Fan/Gas 2.

  2. Tip the anchovies and garlic onto a board. Using the back of a knife, crush the two together to form a rough paste.

  3. In an ovenproof baking dish layer the vegetables – parsnips, potatoes, white onion and then a little of the anchovy and garlic paste. Repeat the layers until done. Pour over the cream.

  4. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the top is golden-brown.
