Chorizo, tomato and chickpea stew

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz chorizo , sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 240g/8½oz canned cherry tomatoes , drained
  • 125g/4½oz canned chickpeas , rinsed and drained
  • 50g/2oz olives , stones removed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 75g/2½oz canned cooked new potatoes , halved
  • 2 tbsp fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Method

  1. Heat a large saucepan and fry the chorizo for 2-3 minutes.

  2. Add the garlic and cook for one minute. Add the cherry tomatoes, chickpeas and olives and cook for 2-3 minutes.

  3. In a separate frying pan, heat the olive oil, add the new potatoes and sauté until golden-brown on all sides. Add the sautéed potatoes to the tomato stew, then pour the mixture into an ovenproof dish.

  4. Preheat the grill to medium.

  5. Place the breadcrumbs and parsley into a small bowl and mix together. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture over the stew, then place under the grill and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until golden-brown on top. Serve in the dish.
