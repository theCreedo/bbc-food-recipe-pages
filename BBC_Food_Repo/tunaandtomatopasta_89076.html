Tuna and tomato pasta

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Brian Turner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ red onion , finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic , chopped
  • 85g/3oz fresh tuna, chopped
  • 1 tomato , chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • small handful chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 125g/4oz penne pasta, cooked according to packet instructions

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan, add the onion and garlic and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until softened but not coloured. Add the tuna and fry for 1-2 minutes. Stir in the chopped tomato, reduce the heat and simmer until the tomato has softened.

  2. Add the soy sauce and a splash of water, stir in the parsley and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and stir well to coat.

  3. Spoon the pasta into a bowl and serve.
