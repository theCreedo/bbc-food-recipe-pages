Freshly-salted cucumbers

Preparation time

overnight

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 jar of salted cucumbers

Dietary

Vegetarian

These salted cucumbers are great for sandwiches and salads.

By The Hairy Bikers
From The Hairy Bikers' Northern Exposure

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp horseradish
  • 2 horseradish leaves
  • 10 small pickling cucumbers , washed
  • 2 tbsp salt

Method

  1. Put the garlic, horseradish and horseradish leaves in the bottom of a large sterilised jar and add the cucumbers.

  2. Add the salt to 2 litres/3½ pints water and mix thoroughly. Pour the salted water over the cucumbers, screw on the lid and leave to rest for 24 hours.

  3. The cucumbers will keep in the fridge for one month.
