Cheese on toast with cranberry and plum chutney

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the cranberry and plum chutney

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ½ onion , chopped
  • 2 plums , stones removed, roughly chopped
  • 1 orange , juice and zest
  • 1 x 190g/7oz jar ready-made cranberry sauce
  • 1 lemon , juice only

For the cheese on toast

  • 100g/3½oz Shropshire blue cheese , grated
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • 2 thick slices homemade bread

Method

  1. For the chutney, heat the oil in a pan. Add the onion and fry for a few minutes until softened, but not coloured.

  2. Add the remaining chutney ingredients and simmer for 5-6 minutes until the plums are soft and the chutney has reduced to a thick consistency.

  3. Leave to cool while you prepare the cheese on toast.

  4. For the cheese on toast, preheat the grill to hot. Mix the cheese, mustard and egg yolk together in a small mixing bowl. Spread the mixture over the slices of bread.

  5. Place onto a baking sheet and cook under the grill for 4-5 minutes until the cheese has melted and is golden-brown.

  6. Slice the toast in half and arrange on a plate. Serve with the cranberry and plum chutney.
