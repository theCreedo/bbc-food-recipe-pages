Steak sandwich

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 slices baguette
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • 170g/6oz rump steak
  • ½ onion , finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 75ml/2½fl oz double cream
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter

Method

  1. Drizzle one side of the baguette slices with one tablespoon of the olive oil and place oiled-side down in a hot griddle pan until toasted. Rub the toasted side with the garlic.

  2. Heat another tablespoon of oil in a non-stick frying pan and fry the steak for 4-6 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking. Set aside to rest in a warm place for a few minutes.

  3. Add the last tablespoon of oil to the pan and fry the onion until soft.

  4. Add the mustard and cream and simmer for 2-3 minutes, until slightly thickened. Season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper and whisk in the butter.

  5. To serve, place the steak between the baguette slices on a plate with a spoonful of the onions.
