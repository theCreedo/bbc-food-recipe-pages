Veal and kidneys with spinach, fries and a veal reduction

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Blend the delicate taste of tender veal with the tang of kidneys and a rich balsamic sauce.

By Matt Tebbutt
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the veal and kidneys

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 x 150g/5½oz veal tail fillet, trimmed, seasoned
  • 300g/10½oz veal kidneys , cleaned and sliced
  • 75g/2½oz unsalted butter
  • 100ml/3½fl oz madeira
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

For the sauté spinach and shoestring fries

  • 100g/3½oz large leaf spinach
  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter
  • 1 large potato , peeled and cut into chips
  • vegetable oil for deep-frying

For the veal reduction

  • 100ml/3½fl oz balsamic vinegar
  • 150ml/5fl oz veal stock
  • 75g/2½oz unsalted butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the dressing

  • 1 shallot , diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 4-6 tbsp chopped parsley

Method

  1. For the veal and kidneys, heat half the oil in a frying pan and once hot cook the veal for 3-4 minutes, or until done to your liking. Set aside to rest.

  2. Heat a clean pan and add the remaining oil and cook the kidneys for 2-3 minutes, then finish with a little butter. Set aside to rest. Put the madeira, thyme and garlic in a clean pan and heat to reduce in volume by half.

  3. Heat the butter in a large sauté pan, add the spinach and a little water and cook until it wilts.

  4. Heat deep-fat fryer to 180C, or half-fill a deep pan with oil and heat to 180C or until a piece of bread dropped into the oil turns golden brown in 15 seconds (Caution: Hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended).

  5. Fry the chips for 3-4 minutes. Drain and season with salt.

  6. For the veal reduction, heat a saucepan and add the vinegar and cook until reduced to half the volume. Add the stock and reduce again to half the volume. Finish with the butter and season with salt and pepper.

  7. For the dressing, place all the ingredients into a medium bowl and mix.

  8. To serve, slice the veal fillet and kidneys. Place the cooked spinach in the centre of the plate and dot with slices of fillet and kidneys. Top with some of the dressing, the veal reduction and the madeira reduction. Mix the rest of the dressing with the warm salted chips and pile on top.
