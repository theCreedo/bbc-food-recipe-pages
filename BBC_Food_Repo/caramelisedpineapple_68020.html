Caramelised pineapple and brioche with spiced ice cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Curtis Stone
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 disc pineapple , core removed
  • 1 disc brioche, same size as pineapple
  • 250g/9oz sugar
  • 250ml/9fl oz double cream
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Method

  1. In a small copper pan place the sugar and heat it. Once it has dissolved. Increase the temperature and allow the sugar to start to colour. Slowly add the cream once the sugar is golden brown.

  2. Add the pineapple to the caramel and cook for 2 minutes on each side. Remove the pineapple and add the brioche and cook for 30 seconds on each side and remove.

  3. Place the pineapple on top of the brioche and place in the centre of the late. Drizzle a little of the sauce around the plate and place a ball of ice cream on top of the pineapple.
