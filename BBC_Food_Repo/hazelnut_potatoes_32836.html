Hazelnut potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian

The crunch of hazelnuts, combined with buttery new potatoes makes for an unusual potato side dish in this easy recipe.

By Nigel Slater
From Nigel and Adam's Farm Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2lb 4oz new potatoes
  • a little olive oil
  • 200g/7oz chopped hazelnuts
  • 150g/5½oz butter

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 150C/300F/Gas 2.

  2. Roughly chop the new potatoes into quarters, but at odd angles.

  3. Heat a dash of olive oil in an oven dish. Add the potatoes and brown them on the hob over a moderate heat.

  4. Roughly chop the hazelnuts.

  5. Heat the butter in a pan until it is almost going a nutty brown colour.

  6. Pour the butter over the potatoes and mix in the hazelnuts.

  7. Bake in the oven for one hour 30 minutes, or until the potatoes are crisp and golden-brown.
