Caramelised peach and cashew nut crumble

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Richard Phillips
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the crumble topping

  • 100g/3½oz plain flour
  • 55g/2oz butter
  • 55g/2oz cashew nuts, crushed
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar

For the filling

  • 1 peach , sliced
  • 75ml/2½fl oz red wine
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar

For the lime yoghurt

  • 3 tbsp Greek-style yoghurt
  • ½ lime , juice only

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the crumble topping, place the flour and butter into a food processor and pulse until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Stir in the crushed nuts and sugar.

  3. For the filling, heat the sliced peach, red wine and sugar in a pan and simmer for 4-5 minutes, until the liquid is slightly reduced.

  4. Pour the peach mixture into an ovenproof dish and top with the crumble mixture.

  5. Bake in the oven for 8-12 minutes, or until the crumble is toasted and golden-brown.

  6. For the lime yoghurt, place the yoghurt in a bowl and stir in the lime juice.

  7. Serve the crumble warm in the dish with a dollop of lime yoghurt.
