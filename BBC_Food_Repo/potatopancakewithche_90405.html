Potato pancake with cheese

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Phil Vickery
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 potato , peeled and chopped
  • 25g/1oz plain flour
  • 50ml/2fl oz milk
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 slices mild cheddar

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4.

  2. Cook the potato in boiling water for 8-10 minutes, or until tender, then drain and mash with a potato masher or fork. Mix in the flour and milk and season, to taste, with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Heat the olive oil in an ovenproof frying pan, shape the potato mixture into four patties and fry for 2-3 minutes on both sides, or until golden-brown and crisp. Place one slice of cheese over each potato patty and place into the oven to cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbling.
