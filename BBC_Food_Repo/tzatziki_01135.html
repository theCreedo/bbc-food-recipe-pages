Tzatziki

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 300g/10oz

Dietary

Vegetarian

Garlic, yoghurt, lemon and fresh mint make a wonderful dip to serve with pitta bread or your favourite kebab.

By Lisa Faulkner

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt , to taste
  • 300g/10oz natural yoghurt
  • 1 lemon , juice only
  • small handful mint leaves, chopped

Method

  1. Crush the garlic with a little salt in a pestle and mortar to make a paste.

  2. Add the yoghurt and lemon juice and stir well. Stir through the mint. Set aside until needed.
