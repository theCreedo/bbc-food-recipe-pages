Homemade coleslaw

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

no cooking required

Dietary

Vegetarian

Crunchy homemade coleslaw with lemon and mustard is one of the healthiest side dishes around.

By Chris Bavin
From Eat Well for Less?

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz low-fat natural yoghurt
  • ½ lemon , juice only
  • 1 tbsp grainy mustard
  • 3 tbsp roughly chopped soft herbs, such as chives, parsley, coriander , mint
  • ¼ white cabbage , finely shredded
  • ¼ red cabbage , finely shredded
  • ½ red onion , thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots , peeled then peeled into ribbons
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Put the yoghurt, lemon juice, mustard and herbs into a large bowl and whisk well to combine. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper.

  2. Add the cabbages, red onion and carrots and mix really well until coated in the mustard mixture.

  3. Ideally, leave to soften and absorb the flavours for 30 minutes before eating.
