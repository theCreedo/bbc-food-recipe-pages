Prawns baked in sea salt with thyme

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

By Mitch Tonks
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 12 raw prawns
  • 500g/1lb 2oz rock salt
  • 8 sprigs fresh thyme
  • lemon wedges, to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7.

  2. Put a layer of rock salt about 1cm/½in thick on a small roasting tray, lay half of the thyme on to it then place the prawns on the herbs. Put the remaining thyme over the top of the prawns then completely bury in the remaining salt.

  3. Sprinkle lightly with water then bake in a preheated oven for 6-7 minutes, crack the salt, remove the prawns and serve with lemon wedges.
