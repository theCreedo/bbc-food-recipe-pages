Stir-fried beansprouts with peppers and herbs

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , sliced
  • ¼ red pepper , chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 150g/5½oz beansprouts
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a wok or large frying pan, add the onion and red pepper and stir fry for 3-4 minutes until softened.

  2. Add the garlic and cook for one minute, then add the beansprouts and cook for a further minute.

  3. Add the herbs, season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper and stir.

  4. To serve, place into a serving dish.
