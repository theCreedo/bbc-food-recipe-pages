Homemade lemonade

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 1 litre

Dietary

Vegetarian

This simple recipe for homemade lemonade is perfect for picnics and barbecues. Add a splash of gin for a grown-up drink.

By Richard Corrigan
From Full on Food

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½ oz fruit sugar
  • 4 lemons , preferably Sicilian, juice only
  • ice
  • about 1 litre/15¼fl oz sparkling mineral water

Method

  1. Put the sugar and lemon juice in a jug.

  2. Add some ice and top up with the mineral water.

  3. Stir to combine.
