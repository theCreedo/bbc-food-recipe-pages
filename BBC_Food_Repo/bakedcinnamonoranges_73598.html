Baked cinnamon oranges and Grand Marnier

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Antony Worrall Thompson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 large oranges
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • ½ cinnamon stick or a little of the powdered spice
  • Grand Marnier

Method

  1. Set oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Peel the fruit with a sharp knife, remove all the pith and cut into slices horizontally about 2.5cm (0.5in) thick.

  3. Bring slices back together to reform the orange. Place on a foil square big enough to encase the orange and sprinkle with sugar.

  4. Break the cinnamon stick in half and sprinkle over the oranges.

  5. Bring together the edges of the foil and wrap around the fruit to form a parcel.

  6. Add a teaspoon of Grand Marnier and scrunch together the foil at the top of each orange.

  7. Bake in the oven for 10-15 minutes, until warmed and fragrant, and serve.
