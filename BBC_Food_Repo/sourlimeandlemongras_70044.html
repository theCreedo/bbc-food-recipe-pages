Sour lime and lemongrass granita

Preparation time

1-2 hours

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

Vegetarian

This is best eaten within a few days, but will keep in the freezer for weeks, though it may need a firm hand to break up the crystals.

By Silvana Franco

Ingredients

  • 175g caster sugar
  • juice and grated rind 8 limes
  • 2 lemon grass stalks, roughly flattened with a rolling pin
  • 150ml vodka
  • 300ml soda water

Method

  1. Place the sugar, lime rind and juice and the lemongrass stalks in a pan with 150ml (¼ pint) water. Heat gently, stirring until the sugar dissolves then simmer gently for 5 minutes then allow to cool completely.

  2. Strain the liquid into a rigid container then stir in the vodka and soda water. Freeze for 2 hours until almost firm then using a fork, break the mixture into large flaky crystals.
