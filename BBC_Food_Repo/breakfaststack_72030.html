Breakfast stack

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Michael Moore
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 slices rye bread
  • 1 tomato
  • 50g/2oz cured ham
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 large pickled gherkin

Method

  1. Take the rye bread and place it on a plate.

  2. Slice tomato, place it on the rye bread.

  3. Take the ham and place it on top of the tomato.

  4. Pre-heat pan with the oil - make sure pan is not too hot, break eggs into frying pan and fry until cooked.

  5. Remove eggs, place on top of ham and serve with the gherkin.
