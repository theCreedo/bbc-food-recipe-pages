Crushed truffle potatoes

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 150g/5½oz new potatoes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • ½ tsp chopped truffle (from a jar)
  • sprig fresh basil , to garnish

Method

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water for 12-15 minutes, or until tender. Drain and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. Add the olive oil and butter and lightly crush with a fork or potato masher. Stir in the chopped truffle.

  3. To serve, place onto a serving plate with a sprig of basil, to garnish.
