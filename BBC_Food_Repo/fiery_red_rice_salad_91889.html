Fiery red rice salad

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 10

Dietary

Vegetarian

This is a hot salad that would be fabulous to serve on a buffet table for a summer supper or barbecue. It is delicious served with chargrilled meats, chicken or fish.

Each serving provides 236kcal, 4g protein, 42g carbohydrate (of which 6g sugars), 5g fat (of which 0.8g saturates), 2.5g fibre and 0.6g salt.

By Mary Berry
From Mary Berry Cooks

Ingredients

  • 450g/1lb Camargue red rice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 carrots
  • 8 spring onions
  • small bunch coriander leaves, chopped

For the dressing

  • 4 limes , finely grated zest and juice only
  • 4 tsp honey
  • 2 red chillies , finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp light olive oil
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Measure the rice in a jug, then place in a pan with double the volume of boiling water. Add the salt. Bring to the boil, then cover and reduce the heat to a low simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, or until the grains are tender but still have a little bite.

  2. Meanwhile, coarsely grate the carrots (use the thick grating blade of a food processor if you have one) and thinly slice the spring onions. Tip into a bowl and add half of the chopped coriander.

  3. For the dressing, make the dressing by mixing all the ingredients together in a jam jar and shake.

  4. Drain the rice if necessary and add to the bowl with the vegetables. Add the dressing, taste and season with more salt and pepper if necessary. Serve with the remaining coriander scattered on top.
