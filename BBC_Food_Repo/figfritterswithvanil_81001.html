Fig fritters with vanilla ice cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Martin
From Stately Suppers

Ingredients

For the batter

  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • fizzy cold water

For the fritters

  • vegetable oil , for frying
  • 8 figs
  • vanilla ice cream , to serve

Method

  1. Make the batter by sieving the plain flour and cornflour together into a large bowl. Whisk in enough fizzy cold water to make a batter the consistency of double cream.

  2. Heat the oil in a deep heavy-bottomed pan. It is ready when a breadcrumb that is dropped in will sizzle gently in it. (CAUTION: hot oil can be dangerous. Do not leave unattended.)

  3. Dip each fig into the batter, then carefully place into the hot oil. Deep fry for 2-3 minutes until crisp and golden brown. Remove using a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen towel.

  4. Serve at once with scoops of vanilla ice cream.
