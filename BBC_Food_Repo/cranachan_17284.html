Cranachan

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 40

Dietary

Vegetarian

This classic Scottish recipe works just as well with raspberries. You'll need a very large bowl to whip the cream, or do it in batches.

By The Hairy Bikers
From Hairy Bikers' Meals on Wheels

Ingredients

  • 1kg/2lb 4oz porridge oats
  • 150g/5½oz caster sugar
  • 3 litres/5¼ pints double cream
  • 3 litres/5¼ pints plain yoghurt
  • 1.8kg/4lb fresh strawberries , hulled, chopped

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas mark 6.

  2. Place the oats on two large baking trays, sprinkle the sugar over them and mix together. Bake in the oven for 7-8 minutes, or until lightly toasted.

  3. Whisk the double cream until it reaches soft peaks. Stir in the yoghurt and almost all of the fresh fruit then fold in the toasted oats.

  4. Place in serving dishes, top with the remaining fruit and chill until ready to serve.
