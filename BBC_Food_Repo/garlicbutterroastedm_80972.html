Garlic butter roasted mussels

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic , finely chopped
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter , softened
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander , plus extra to garnish
  • 6 green-lipped mussels , de-bearded and cleaned, one shell removed
  • 75g/3oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ lime , cut into wedges

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic, butter and coriander.

  3. Place the mussels on a small baking tray. Divide the butter between the mussels, then sprinkle over the breadcrumbs and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Bake in the oven for 5 minutes.

  4. To serve, transfer to a warm plate and sprinkle with coriander and lime wedges.
