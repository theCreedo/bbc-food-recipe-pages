Port and Stilton soufflé

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 85g/3oz Stilton
  • 2 tbsp port
  • 3 tbsp double cream
  • 1 free-range egg yolk
  • 3 free-range egg whites
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Place the Stilton, port and cream into a small non-stick ovenproof frying pan. Heat gently until the cheese has melted and the mixture is smooth.

  3. Remove from the heat and stir in the egg yolk.

  4. In a bowl, whisk the egg whites with the lemon juice until stiff peaks form when the whisk is removed. Fold the egg whites into the cheese mixture.

  5. Transfer to the oven and bake for 6-8 minutes, or until well risen and lightly golden on top.
