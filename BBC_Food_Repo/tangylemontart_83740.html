Tangy lemon tart

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 6

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Elizabeth Guy
From Countryfile Summer Diaries

Ingredients

  • 200g/8oz readymade shortcrust pastry
  • 8 free-range eggs
  • 320g/11oz caster sugar
  • 4 large lemons , juice and rind
  • 290ml/10fl oz/½ pint double cream

To serve

  • icing sugar , for dusting
  • single cream
  • raspberries

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F/Gas 5.

  2. Roll out the pastry and use to line a large quiche dish (25cm/10in diameter). Line with tin foil and fill with dried beans or rice to hold the base in place.

  3. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and baking beans and bake for a further 5 minutes.

  4. Reduce the oven temperature to 150C/300F/Gas 2.

  5. In a mixing bowl, lightly beat the eggs and the sugar (do not whisk). Add the lemon juice and rind and stir. Finally add the cream and stir thoroughly but again, do not whisk.

  6. Transfer the cream mixture into the pastry case and cook in the centre of the oven for about 35 minutes until set (the mixture should set gently like a blancmange and be firm to the touch).

  7. Allow to cool completely, dust with icing sugar and serve with pouring cream and raspberries.
