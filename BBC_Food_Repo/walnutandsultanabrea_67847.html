Walnut and sultana bread with almonds

Preparation time

1-2 hours

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes 1 loaf

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Hollywood
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz strong wholemeal flour
  • 110g/4oz strong white flour
  • 30g/1oz fresh yeast
  • 15g/½oz salt
  • 255ml/9oz water
  • 85g/3oz sultanas
  • 85g/3oz walnuts
  • handful flaked almonds
  • Stilton , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6. Mix the flour, yeast, salt and water in a bowl with your hands until it becomes a soft, pliable dough.

  2. Place the dough on to a lightly floured, flat surface and knead for five minutes. Put the dough back in the bowl and leave to rise for an hour.

  3. Bring the dough out and mix the sultanas and walnuts into it.

  4. Shape the dough into a loaf, and roll the loaf in flaked almonds.

  5. With a sharp knife, cut a line down the centre of the loaf.

  6. Bake for 30 minutes, or until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack, serve toasted or with Stilton on the side.
