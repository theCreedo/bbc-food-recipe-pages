Pineapple tarte tatins with black pepper caramel

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Makes 2

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Martin
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the tatin

  • 110g/4oz caster sugar
  • 20g/3½oz butter
  • 2 rings pineapple
  • 150g/5oz ready-made puff pastry , rolled to 5mm/¼in thick

For the black pepper caramel

  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 75ml/3fl oz double cream
  • 2 tbsp rum
  • ¼ tsp cracked black pepper
  • coconut ice cream , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. Divide the sugar evenly between two small ovenproof blini pans over a low heat. Cook without stirring, until the sugar has melted and caramelised in both pans, turning golden brown.

  3. Remove from the heat and place half of the butter into each pan. Stir gently to combine.

  4. Place a pineapple ring into the caramel in each pan.

  5. Place the rolled out pastry onto a clean, floured surface and cut out two circles slightly larger than the circumference of the blini pans.

  6. Cover each pan with a pastry round, pushing the edge down around the pineapple ring. Transfer the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, until the pastry is golden brown and cooked through. Remove and allow to cool slightly.

  7. Meanwhile, for the black pepper caramel, place the sugar into a saucepan and heat gently without stirring until it has melted and turned a golden caramel colour.

  8. Remove from the heat and place the base of the pan into cold water to prevent the sugar cooking further.

  9. Add the double cream, rum and black pepper and stir to combine.

  10. To serve, turn each tarte tatin out into the centre of a plate. Spoon the black pepper caramel around the tart and top with a spoonful of coconut ice cream.
