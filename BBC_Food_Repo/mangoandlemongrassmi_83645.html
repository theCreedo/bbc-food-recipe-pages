Mango and lemongrass milkshake

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 4

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Merrett
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks lemongrass
  • 280ml/10fl oz; pint milk
  • 2 ripe mangoes, skinned and stones removed
  • 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream

Method

  1. Smash the lemongrass in a pestle and mortar until its aromas are released.

  2. Boil half of the milk and add the lemongrass. When the infused milk has cooled to room temperature, remove the lemongrass and discard.

  3. Place the milk in a blender, together with the other ingredients and the remaining cold milk. Whiz until smooth.

  4. Pour into a tall glass to serve.
