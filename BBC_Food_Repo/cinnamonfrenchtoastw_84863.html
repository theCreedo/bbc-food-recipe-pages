Cinnamon French toast with peaches

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By James Tanner
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the French toast

  • 100ml/3½fl oz milk
  • 1 free-range egg
  • pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 soda farl, cut in half
  • 30g/1oz butter

For the peaches

  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 1 vanilla pod , cut in half
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 1 peach , cut into wedges
  • honey , to drizzle

Method

  1. For the French toast, place the milk, egg and cinnamon into a bowl and mix well.

  2. Dip the soda farl halves into the egg mixture until soaked.

  3. Heat the butter in a frying pan and cook the soda farls for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden-brown. Set aside and keep warm.

  4. For the peaches, place the caster sugar, split vanilla pod and white wine into a saucepan and bring to the boil. Add the peach pieces to the pan and simmer for 4-5 minutes, or until the peach is tender.

  5. To serve, place the French toast onto a serving plate. Remove the peaches from the syrup with a slotted spoon and place on top of the toast, drizzled with honey.
