Mackerel pâté

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 can mackerel fillets, drained
  • 30g/1oz butter
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ lemon , juice only
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Place all of the ingredients together in a food processor, pulse until smooth and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

  2. To serve, transfer the mackerel mixture to a small serving bowl with toasted bread or crackers on the side.
