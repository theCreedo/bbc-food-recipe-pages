Fresh papardelle pasta with spinach, anchovy and tomato sauce

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Gino D'Acampo
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the papardelle pasta

  • 200g/7oz plain flour , plus extra for dusting
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • pinch salt

For the sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion , finely chopped
  • 8 cherry tomatoes , halved
  • 5 canned anchovies, drained and chopped
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 1 handful baby spinach leaves

To serve

  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 fresh basil leaves

Method

  1. For the pasta, place all of the pasta ingredients into a food processor and pulse until the mixture comes together to form a dough. Tip out onto a floured work surface and knead lightly, then roll out very thinly (use a pasta machine if you have one) and cut into strips using a pasta-cutting wheel.

  2. Cook the pasta in boiling water for 1-2 minutes, or until cooked but still al dente, then drain and set aside.

  3. For the sauce, heat the olive oil in a pan and gently fry the onion for 2-3 minutes, then stir in the tomatoes, anchovies and wine. Simmer for 3-4 minutes, or until reduced slightly, then stir in the spinach to wilt.

  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, mix well to coat, season with black pepper and garnish with the fresh basil leaves.
