Saturday Kitchen Sling

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Dietary

Vegetarian

Make as much or as little as desired, the choice is yours!

By Peter Richards
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the cocktail

  • 2 parts gin
  • 2 parts sweet red vermouth
  • 2 parts orange liqueur
  • 1 part Campari
  • 2 parts pink grapefruit juice
  • 10 parts cloudy lemonade

To serve

  • strawberries , quartered
  • mint sprigs
  • 1 grapefruit , peel only
  • ice

Method

  1. Mix all the alcoholic components together with the pink grapefruit juice in a large jug. Pour in the cloudy lemonade and stir.

  2. Fill serving glasses with ice and pour over the cocktail.

  3. Garnish with two quarters of a strawberry and a mint sprig per drink. Top with a twist of grapefruit peel.
