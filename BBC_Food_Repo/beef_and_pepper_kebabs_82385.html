Beef and pepper kebabs

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 4

These skewers are very easy to make and packed with flavour.
This meal is low calorie and provides 421 kcal, 27.8g protein, 32.3g carbohydrate (of which 5.5g sugars), 20.6g fat (of which 2.8g saturates), 1.6g fibre and 0.4g salt per portion.

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz steak, rump or topside
  • 1 red pepper , cored and deseeded
  • 1 green pepper , cored and deseeded
  • 1 tsp crushed coriander seeds
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 15g/½oz chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 red chilli , deseeded and chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 4 chapatis
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Method

  1. Soak 8 wooden skewers in warm water.

  2. Cut the beef and peppers into 2.5cm/1in cubes.

  3. Mix together the coriander seeds, 2 tablespoons of oil and half the chopped coriander in a bowl and season to taste. Add the beef and peppers and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for up to 1 hour.

  4. Mix together the remaining coriander and oil with the chilli, garlic and lime juice to make a dressing, season to taste and set aside.

  5. Preheat the grill to a hot setting. Thread the beef and peppers on to the skewers. Place the beef skewers on a foil lined tray and grill for 15 minutes, turning often and basting with the juices. Warm the chapatis under the grill.

  6. Put two skewers on each chapati, drizzle over the coriander dressing and serve.
