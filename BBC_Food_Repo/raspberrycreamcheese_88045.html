Raspberry, cream cheese and amaretti trifle

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 1 lemon , juice and zest only
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 10 amaretti biscuits
  • 85g/3oz cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp double cream
  • 100g/3½oz frozen raspberries, defrosted
  • 1 tsp icing sugar

Method

  1. Pour the white wine, juice of half a lemon and sugar into a small pan over a high heat. Boil for five minutes or until the liquid has reduced by half and resembles a syrup. Remove from the heat and leave to cool slightly.

  2. Dip the amaretti biscuits into the syrup and place half of them into the base of a serving glass.

  3. Mix the cream cheese, icing sugar, juice and zest of the remaining half of lemon and double cream together in a clean bowl. Spoon half of this mixture over the top of the dipped amaretti biscuits in the glass.

  4. Place the remaining dipped amaretti biscuits over the top of the cream cheese layer, then spoon the remaining cream cheese mixture on top.

  5. Place the raspberries and icing sugar into a food processor and blend until smooth. Pour the raspberry sauce over the dessert in the glass and serve.
