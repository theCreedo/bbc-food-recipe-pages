Savoury bites

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Dietary

Vegetarian

These savoury biscuits take no time to make and are delicious alongside homemade soup or served with cheese.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 100g/3½oz self-raising flour
  • 25g/1oz soft butter
  • 1 tsp chopped chives
  • 25g/1oz feta , crumbled
  • 50ml/2fl oz whole milk

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C/425F/Gas 7. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper.

  2. Rub the flour and butter together in a bowl until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Stir in the chives and feta. Add the milk and stir together to form a dough.

  3. Tip the dough out onto a floured work surface and roll out to a thickness of 0.5cm/¼ in thickness.

  4. Using a biscuit cutter, cut out shapes and place onto the baking tray. Re-roll any leftover dough to make more biscuits.

  5. Bake in the oven for 8–10 minutes, or until risen and golden-brown.
