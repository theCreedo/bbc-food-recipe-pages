Sesame-crusted Japanese-style leeks

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Ross Burden
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 leek , sliced
  • splash rice wine/ white wine
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a wok.

  2. Add the leek and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes or until cooked through.

  3. Add the wine, garlic, salt and sesame seeds. Finish cooking for a further two minutes.

  4. Serve hot.
