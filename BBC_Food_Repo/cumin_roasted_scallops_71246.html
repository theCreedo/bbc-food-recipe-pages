Cumin roasted scallops with a salad of honey glazed chicken wings, apple, celeriac and hazelnuts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

1 to 2 hours

Serves

Serves 4

A gourmet way with chicken wings. This is an intricate dish but each part stands on its own so don't be afraid to try individual elements.

By Kenny Atkinson
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

For the glazed chicken wings

  • 10 cornfed chicken wings , topped and tailed
  • 500g/1lb 2oz duck fat
  • ½ garlic bulb
  • 6 sprigs thyme
  • 1 lemon , zest and juice
  • 75g/2½oz butter
  • 75g/2½oz clear honey
  • salt

For the roasted hazelnuts

  • 50g/1¾oz hazelnuts , peeled
  • 10g/¼oz unsalted butter

For the celeriac purée

  • 250g/9oz celeriac , peeled and chopped
  • 1 banana shallot , peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • 75g/2½oz unsalted butter
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 50ml/2fl oz double cream

For the apple and vanilla chutney

  • 2 Granny Smith apples , peeled and finely chopped
  • 100ml/3½fl oz cider vinegar
  • ¼ vanilla pod , seeds only
  • 200g/7oz caster sugar

For the cumin roasted scallops

  • 12 scallops , removed from their shells, cleaned and roe removed
  • 2g ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • salt
  • 50g/1¾oz unsalted butter
  • 1 lemon , juice only

To serve

  • hazelnut oil
  • 1 Granny Smith apple , cut into fine matchsticks
  • 2 tbsp red shiso cress
  • 1 tbsp coriander cress

Method

  1. For the glazed chicken wings, preheat the oven to 150C/300F/Gas 2.

  2. Place the chicken wings, duck fat, garlic, thyme and lemon zest in an ovenproof dish and cover with foil. Place in the oven for an hour. Leave to cool before removing the centre bone and trimming the meat.

  3. Heat a large frying pan and add the butter. Once hot, add the chicken wings skin-side down and cook until golden-brown. Add the honey and baste to form a sticky glaze. Season with salt and lemon juice.

  4. For the roasted hazelnuts, heat a frying pan and add the hazelnuts and butter. Slowly roast until golden-brown. Once cool, cut in half.

  5. For the celeriac purée, place all the ingredients apart from the double cream, into a large saucepan with 250ml/9fl oz water. Bring to the boil and reduce until the water and butter has emulsified to a third of the volume.

  6. Place into a food processor and blend. Season with salt and pepper and pass through a fine sieve. Finish by mixing in the double cream. Keep warm until ready to serve.

  7. For the apple and vanilla chutney, place all the ingredients with 50ml/2fl oz water into a medium saucepan and heat gently until all the liquid has absorbed into the apples.

  8. For the cumin roasted scallops, dust the cumin over one side of the scallops. Heat a large frying pan and add the oil. Once hot, fry the scallops cumin-side down until golden-brown then season with salt.

  9. Add the butter and turn the scallops over. Pour in the lemon juice and baste the scallops. Finish with a little salt.

  10. To serve, place a spoonful of celeriac purée on each plate and using a small palette knife swipe the purée to form a smear.

  11. Arrange three scallops over the purée and arrange the chicken wings in between the scallops. Spoon a little of the apple chutney next to the wings.

  12. Scatter over the hazelnuts and place the apple matchsticks over the wings and scallops. Finish with red shiso cress and coriander cress and dress with a little hazelnut oil.
