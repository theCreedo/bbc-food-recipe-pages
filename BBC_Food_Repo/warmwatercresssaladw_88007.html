Warm watercress salad with honey-roasted figs, Stilton and walnuts

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

10 to 30 mins

Serves

Serves 1

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Paul Rankin
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the watercress salad

  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 100g/3½oz watercress

For the roasted onion

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ red onion , finely sliced
  • 1 tsp soft brown sugar
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

For the roasted figs

  • 2 figs , halved
  • 1 tbsp clear honey

For the toppings

  • 30g/1oz Stilton , crumbled
  • 2 tbsp shelled walnuts

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.

  2. For the watercress salad, place the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper into a large bowl and whisk to combine. Add the watercress and stir to coat.

  3. For the roasted onion, heat the olive oil in a pan, add the red onion and fry over a gentle heat for five minutes until softened.

  4. Add the sugar and cook for one minute. Add the balsamic vinegar and cook for three minutes. Set aside to cool slightly, then add to the bowl with the watercress.

  5. For the roasted figs, place the figs cut-side up on a baking sheet and drizzle with honey. Place in the oven for five minutes, or until softened and darker in colour.

  6. To serve, spoon the watercress salad onto a serving plate. Place the roasted figs on top and scatter with crumbled Stilton and walnuts.
