Cheaty peach ice cream

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Serves 4-6

Dietary

Vegetarian
By Hugh Fearnley-Whittingstall

Ingredients

  • 1 large (800g/1¾lb) tin peaches in syrup
  • ½ lemon , juice only
  • 500ml/16fl oz double cream

Method

  1. Empty the contents of the can and the lemon juice into a liquidiser and blend until smooth.

  2. Stir in the cream. Pour into an ice cream machine, churn and freeze.
