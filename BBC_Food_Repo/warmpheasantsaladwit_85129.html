Warm pheasant salad with wild mushroom dressing

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 2

By Nick Nairn
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

For the pheasant

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 pheasant legs
  • 2 garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the salad

  • 100g/3½oz spinach
  • 1 orange , peeled, segmented
  • ½ peach , peeled and chopped

For the wild mushroom dressing

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g/1¾oz trompette de la mort mushrooms, or other wild mushrooms , cleaned and finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp white wine

Method

  1. For the pheasant, heat the oil in a small frying pan over a medium heat and place the pheasant, garlic and thyme into the pan. Season well with salt and freshly ground black pepper and cook for 15 minutes, turning once, or until cooked to your liking.

  2. For the salad, place the spinach, orange segments and peach pieces into a large bowl and mix well.

  3. For the wild mushroom dressing, heat the olive oil in a small saucepan, add the mushrooms and cook gently for 2-3 minutes.

  4. Add the wine, turn up the heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until reduced by half.

  5. To serve, place the salad onto serving plates, top with the pheasant legs and drizzle over the mushroom dressing.
