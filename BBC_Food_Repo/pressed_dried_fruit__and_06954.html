Pressed dried fruit and nut energy bars

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 12

Dietary

Vegetarian

Don't want to spend all your money on snack bars? Just throw a few ingredients into your food processor and voila!

Ingredients

  • 400g/14oz soft pitted dates
  • 300g/10½oz cashew nut pieces
  • 4 tbsp cacao or cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla essence

Method

  1. Grease and line a 20x20cm/8x8in baking tray.

  2. Put all the ingredients into a food processor and blend until the cashew nuts become tiny pieces.

  3. Remove the lid and add 1 tablespoon of water and blend again. If the mixture is not already sticking together, add another spoon of water and blend again. Repeat until the mixture starts to stick together.

  4. Using a spoon or spatula, transfer the mixture into the lined tray. Press the mixture into the tin and put in the fridge to set for at least an hour.

  5. Remove from the fridge and slice into 12 bars or smaller chunks. Wrap individually in greaseproof paper and keep in the fridge until you need a healthy snack on the go.
