Torta nera (chocolate walnut tart)

Preparation time

30 mins to 1 hour

Cooking time

30 mins to 1 hour

Serves

Serves 8

Dietary

Vegetarian

Crumbly pastry is filled with a mixture of chocolate, espresso, walnuts and amaretti biscuits to make this rich and decadent Italian dessert. Bellissimo!

By The Massaccesi Family
From The Big Family Cooking Showdown

Ingredients

For the sweet pastry

  • 200g/7oz plain flour , plus extra for dusting
  • pinch of salt
  • 100g/3½oz butter , chilled, cut into small pieces
  • 50g/1¾oz caster sugar
  • 2 free-range egg yolks

For the filling

  • 230g/8oz walnuts , plus extra to decorate
  • 200g/7oz caster sugar
  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 50g/1¾oz butter , melted
  • 65g/2¼oz amaretti biscuits, roughly crushed
  • 75g/2½oz dark chocolate , grated
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee powder, dissolved in a little hot water
  • 50ml/2fl oz almond liqueur
  • icing sugar , to decorate
  • mascarpone , to serve

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6 and butter a 23cm/9in deep, fluted, loose-bottomed tart tin.

  2. To make the pastry, put the flour and salt in a bowl and rub in the butter with your fingertips until the mixture resembles coarse breadcrumbs. Add the sugar and mix in the egg yolks. Bring the dough together without kneading and wrap in cling film. Put it in the fridge for 30 minutes.

  3. Roll out the pastry on a lightly floured surface and use it to line the prepared tart tin. Cover the pastry with greaseproof paper and fill with baking beans. Bake blind for 10–12 minutes. Remove the baking beans and greaseproof paper and return to the oven for 5–6 minutes, or until lightly golden. Leave to cool.

  4. Reduce the oven temperature to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4.

  5. To make the filling, blend the walnuts with 100g/3½oz sugar in a food processor until finely ground.

  6. Whisk the eggs with the remaining sugar. Carefully stir in the melted butter, ground walnut mixture, amaretti biscuits, chocolate, flour, coffee and almond liqueur. Mix well.

  7. Spoon the filling into the tart case. Decorate with the whole walnuts and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes.

  8. Serve warm with a sprinkling of icing sugar and a small scoop of mascarpone.
