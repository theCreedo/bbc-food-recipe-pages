Savoury cheesecake

Preparation time

1-2 hours

Cooking time

no cooking required

Serves

Makes 4

Try these individual savoury cheesecakes as a quick and easy starter recipe. They can be prepared ahead of time, too.

By Kate Morris and Sally Brown
From I Can Cook on the Go

Ingredients

  • 4 oatcakes
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter , melted
  • small handful pumpkin seeds
  • 75g/3oz cream cheese
  • 75g/3oz cottage cheese
  • ¼ lemon , juice only
  • 6 cherry tomatoes , chopped
  • pinch paprika
  • 1 slice ham , chopped

Method

  1. Put the oatcakes into a sandwich bag and crush with the end of a rolling pin to make breadcrumbs. Pour the crumbs into a mixing bowl and stir in the pumpkin seeds and melted butter.

  2. Divide the mixture between four small glasses and press down gently.

  3. Mix the cream cheese and cottage cheese in a bowl, then stir in the lemon juice, tomatoes, paprika and ham.

  4. Divide the cheese mixture between the four glasses and smooth the top. Cover with cling film and chill in the fridge for 1-2 hours.
